## 2026-06-13 | milestone | Full ingest project complete (12 clusters, 124 sources, ~196 pages)
- Summary: The full wiki-ingest project for this vault is now complete. Across 12 clusters spanning 124 source documents, the vault grew to roughly 196 wiki pages covering addresses c-000001 to c-000177, building a comprehensive knowledge base on clinical laboratory quality management and accreditation — ISO 15189:2022/2024, ACCREDIA, SIPMeL clinical guidance and Choosing Wisely campaigns across ~10 study groups, EU MDR/IVDR, and the family of generic UNI organizational standards (quality, risk, continuity, safety, knowledge, asset, facility, and maintenance management).
- Key insight: The vault now models laboratory quality management as an integrated system — ISO 15189's clause 8 management-system requirements connect outward to the generic UNI/ISO management-system family (9001, 9004, 31000, 22301, 45001/45002, 29995, 30401, 55001/55002, 41001/41011/41012, and maintenance standards), all hubbed through the central Sistema di Gestione per la Qualità concept.
