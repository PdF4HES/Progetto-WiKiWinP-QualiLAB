# Improved bounds on the number of holomorphic maps between compact Riemann surfaces

**Autori:** Masaharu Tanabe · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21137) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21137)

## Abstract

New upper bounds for the number of nonconstant holomorphic maps depending only on the genus, improving previously known bounds. The proof studies pullbacks of holomorphic differentials using techniques from the geometry of numbers and the theory of Jacobian varieties.
