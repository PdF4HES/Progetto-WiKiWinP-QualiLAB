# Distributed Stochastic Graph Algorithms

**Autori:** Keren Censor-Hillel, Aditi Dudeja, George Giakkoupis · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21248) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21248)

## Abstract

Studies stochastic graph optimization in a distributed setting where vertices know only their own edges in a realized random subgraph. Establishes that distributed stochastic algorithms can overcome known lower bounds with fast approximation algorithms for matching and covering problems.
