# Serre functors and local duality for affine quotients

**Autori:** Ivan Noden · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21290) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21290)

## Abstract

Study of Serre functors of categories of quasicoherent sheaves on stacks of the form Spec A/G where G is a reductive group acting on Spec A with a unique closed orbit. The Serre functor is given by tensoring with the local cohomology of ω_Y at the unique closed orbit. Development of analogues of the Matlis and local duality theorems for local rings.
