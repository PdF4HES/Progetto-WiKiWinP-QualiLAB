# Weighted Uniform Endpoint Majorants for Integrals Involving Modified Bessel Functions

**Autori:** Yaoran Yang, Yutong Zhang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20983) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20983)

## Abstract

An affirmative full-range solution to Gaunt's 2019 Open Problem 2.10 is provided. The problem questions whether reciprocal-power integrals with modified Bessel functions are uniformly bounded by explicit constant multiples throughout the natural parameter range. The weighted theorem yields shifted-order and moment estimates, applies to approximate power weights and monotone regularly varying amplitudes, and provides two-sided estimates under reversed comparisons.
