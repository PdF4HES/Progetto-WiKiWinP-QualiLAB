# Efficient purely organic phosphorescent emitters for programmable luminescent tags

**Autori:** Uliana Tsiko et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21022) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21022)

## Abstract

Systematic exploration of donor-acceptor and donor-acceptor-donor organic phosphorescent emitters using phenoxathiine and thianthrene as donors with benzophenone or pyridine as acceptors. Results demonstrate structure-property relationships where emission wavelength is tunable through donor moiety, with sky-blue emission at 480 nm for phenoxathiine-based and green at 520 nm for thianthrene-based emitters.
