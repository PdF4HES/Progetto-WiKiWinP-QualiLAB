# Semi-Bousfield classes and nonmonotone perversities

**Autori:** Dolors Herbera, Michal Hrbek, Giovanna Le Gros · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30262) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30262)

## Abstract

Introduces semi-Bousfield classes in rigidly-compactly generated tensor triangulated categories. The stratification bijection naturally extends to an assignment which takes a (not necessarily monotone) perversity on X to a semi-Bousfield class.
