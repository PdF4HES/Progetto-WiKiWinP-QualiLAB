# Neural-Behavioral Representation of Natural Whole-body Movement in Monkeys

**Autori:** Jieshi He, Puzhe Li, Yanan Sui et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29355) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29355)

## Abstract

This work presents a neural-behavioral framework for freely moving monkeys, combining large-scale epidural cortical signals with synchronized multi-view motion capture. An autoencoder-decoder model reconstructed whole-body kinematics and decoded realistic whole-body movements conditioned on neural signals, providing a proof-of-concept approach for decoding natural movements using intracranial neural activity.
