# Stabilization theorem and symmetric structure of Cuntz-Pimsner algebras

**Autori:** Miho Mukohara, Yuhei Suzuki · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21128) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21128)

## Abstract

Establishes crossed product decomposition for stabilized Cuntz-Pimsner algebras, extending Cuntz's classical results. Characterizes simplicity, classifies ideals and weights, studies quasi-free actions on Cuntz algebras.
