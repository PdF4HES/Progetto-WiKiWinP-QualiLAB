# Essentially singular limits of Jacobi operators and applications to higher-order squeezing

**Autori:** Felix Fischer, Daniel Burgarth, Davide Lonigro · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21355) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21355)

## Abstract

Analyzes singular limiting behavior of Jacobi operators using discrete WKB and Airy asymptotics, with applications to quantum optical squeezing operators and their self-adjoint extensions.
