# Quantum Spin-5/2 Blume-Capel Model in a Random Transverse-Crystalline Field Anisotropy

**Autori:** Claudio M. Salgado, Karollaine C. Leite, Thiago M. Tunes et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30125) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30125)

## Abstract

Study of thermodynamic properties in quantum Blume-Capel model with spin 5/2 under transverse and random crystalline fields. Mean-field analysis reveals second-order phase transitions in most parameter ranges, with first-order transitions for certain anisotropy values.
