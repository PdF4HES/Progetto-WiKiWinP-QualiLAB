# Universal interface fluctuations in absorbing-state phase transitions

**Autori:** Yohsuke T. Fukai, Keiichi Tamai, Tetsuya Hiraiwa · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.17781) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.17781)

## Abstract

Numerical study of (1+1)-dimensional interfaces in (2+1)-dimensional models reveals a universal crossover from absorbing phase transition fluctuations to Kardar-Parisi-Zhang growth. Discrete and continuum models show identical fluctuation properties when time and length are rescaled by correlation parameters. Results demonstrate that KPZ growth parameters depend solely on fundamental absorbing phase transition properties.
