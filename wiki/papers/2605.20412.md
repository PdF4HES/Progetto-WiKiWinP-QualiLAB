# Brascamp-Lieb inequalities for fractal dimensions

**Autori:** Jonathan M. Fraser · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20412) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20412)

## Abstract

The Brascamp-Lieb inequality from functional analysis is applied to establish inequalities for upper box, packing, and Assouad dimensions of fractal sets in terms of projection dimensions. Such inequalities do not hold for Hausdorff or lower box dimensions. Applications include exceptional set estimates for orthogonal projections and sharp dimension estimates for constrained sumsets.
