# Intersecting Dense Automata

**Autori:** Dmitry Chistikov, Neha Rino · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20421) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20421)

## Abstract

Alternative constructions for NFA intersection with improved transition complexity. The approach achieves O(mn) transitions compared to Θ(m²) for classical Cartesian products, yielding faster algorithms for emptiness decision.
