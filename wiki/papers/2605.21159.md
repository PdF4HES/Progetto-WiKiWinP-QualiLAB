# Mirror Symmetry of the Affine Toda Systems

**Autori:** Xin Jin, Zhiwei Yun · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21159) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21159)

## Abstract

Proof of homological mirror symmetry between the wrapped Fukaya category of the affine Toda system for a complex reductive group G and coherent sheaves on the regular centralizer group scheme for the Langlands dual group. Interpreted as a geometric Langlands equivalence for P^1 with mildest wild ramification at 0 and infinity.
