# Synergistic approach to probing the dynamics and mechanics of patchy soft matter

**Autori:** Md Mozakker H. Shojib, Asier C. Monasterio, Emanuele Locatelli et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29967) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29967)

## Abstract

The research presents a synergistic strategy to explore the parameter space that comprises simulations, experimental rheology, and machine learning. The team investigated DNA-based self-assembled fluids, using coarse-grained simulations benchmarked against experimental data to predict rheological behavior. They employed Gaussian Process Regression and active learning to navigate the design space with high predictive accuracy, creating a framework intended for iterative deployment in rational design and accelerated discovery of soft matter suspensions.
