# Existence of Minimal Homotopies for Immersed Planar Curves

**Autori:** Lia Buchbinder, Yunjia Kou, Bala Krishnamoorthy et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29474) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29474)

## Abstract

We study the existence of area-minimizing homotopies between homotopic curves in the plane using variational and geometric methods adapted from Plateau problem theory.
