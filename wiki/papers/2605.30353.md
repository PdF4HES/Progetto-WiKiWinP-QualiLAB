# Physics Is All You Need? A Case Study in Physicist-Supervised AI Development of Scientific Software

**Autori:** Nhat-Minh Nguyen · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30353) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30353)

## Abstract

Case study of a physicist supervising an AI coding agent over 12 work days and 57 sessions to build CLAX-PT, a differentiable one-loop perturbation theory module in JAX. Documents 15 supervision events, analyzing how the agent resolved issues autonomously, with domain knowledge, or failed when treating symptom reduction as root-cause resolution.
