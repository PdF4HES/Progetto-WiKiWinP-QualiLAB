# Ridge Regression from Poisson Resetting: A Renewal Perspective on Spectral Regularization

**Autori:** Petar Jolakoski · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30059) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30059)

## Abstract

Connection between stochastic resetting from non-equilibrium physics and ridge regularization in learning. Demonstrates how resetting to origin at rate r produces the ridge estimator with penalty λ=r through renewal process interpretation.
