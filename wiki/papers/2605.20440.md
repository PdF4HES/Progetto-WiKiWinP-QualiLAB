# Group-Algebraic Tensors: Provably-optimal Equivariant Learning and Physical Symmetry Discovery

**Autori:** Paulina Hoyos et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20440) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20440)

## Abstract

Introduction of the star_G tensor algebra where finite groups define multiplication rules, making equivariance intrinsic. Provides an Eckart-Young optimality guarantee for the star_G-SVD and demonstrates recovery of Wigner-Eckart selection rules from molecular geometry data.
