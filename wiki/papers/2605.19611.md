# Inverse Design of Metasurface based Absorbers using Physics Guided Conditional Diffusion Models

**Autori:** Vineetha Joy et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19611) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19611)

## Abstract

Proposes physics-guided diffusion framework for metasurface design achieving average spectral mean squared error of 0.0006. Generates practical designs in 30 seconds versus months for conventional methods.
