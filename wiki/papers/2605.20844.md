# Brunnian braids and the inclusion from double shuffle Lie algebra to Kashiwara-Vergne Lie algebra

**Autori:** Muze Ren · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20844) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20844)

## Abstract

Proves that the double shuffle Lie algebra with infinitesimal hexagon equation injects into symmetric Kashiwara-Vergne Lie algebra using brunnian braids group inclusions, establishing connections between pentagon equations, stuffle coproducts, and necklace cobrackets.
