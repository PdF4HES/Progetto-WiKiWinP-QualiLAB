# Periodic and quasiperiodic traveling waves in nonlinear lattices with odd elasticity

**Autori:** Andrus Giraldo, Stefan Ruschel, Behrooz Yousefzadeh · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18997) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.18997)

## Abstract

Odd elasticity in active material models breaks action-reaction symmetry, producing novel wave behavior. This work investigates traveling waves in nonlinear lattices with asymmetric nonreciprocal elastic coupling. The study demonstrates existence of periodic and quasiperiodic waves, analyzes spectral stability using master stability framework, and identifies Eckhaus instability onset, establishing a unified framework for wave propagation.
