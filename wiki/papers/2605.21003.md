# Thermodynamic and structural behavior of one-dimensional divalent patchy hard rods

**Autori:** Ana M. Montero et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21003) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21003)

## Abstract

Investigation of thermodynamic and structural properties of divalent patchy hard rods in one dimension using attractive square-well patches at rod tips. While Wertheim's theory becomes exact in the sticky limit, finite-range interactions produce richer structural behavior including monotonic and oscillatory pair correlation function decay separated by the Fisher-Widom line.
