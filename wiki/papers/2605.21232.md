# On the nonnegative rank of positive operators

**Autori:** Roman Drnovšek, Marko Kandić · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21232) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21232)

## Abstract

The concept of a nonnegative rank of a positive operator T:X→Y between ordered vector spaces is introduced. In the case of nonnegative matrices, the definition agrees with the standard one. Under natural and mild assumptions on the cone Y_+, it is proven that the nonnegative rank and the rank agree whenever the rank is at most two. An example of a positive rank-three operator on C[0,1] with an infinite nonnegative rank is provided.
