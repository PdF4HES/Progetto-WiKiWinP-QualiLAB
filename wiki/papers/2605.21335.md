**Autori:** Yasmine Briefs et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21335) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21335)

## Abstract

Extends the two-watched literal scheme from propositional CDCL solvers to first-order logic, efficiently detecting propagating and false clauses with proven soundness and completeness.
