# The Team Order Problem: Maximizing the Probability of Matching Being Large Enough

**Autori:** Haris Aziz et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21234) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21234)

## Abstract

Addresses matching problems in team competitions by developing a polynomial-time approximation scheme to compute optimal player orderings. Provides tractability results for special cases and analytical bounds on winning probability differences.
