# Diagrammatic Multiplet-Sum Method (MSM) Density-Functional Theory (DFT): III. Inclusion of Relaxation and Application to LiH

**Autori:** Mark E Casida, Abraham Ponra, Gadzikano Munyuki et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29924) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29924)

## Abstract

The work extends the multiplet sum method to include nondynamic correlation through nonorthogonal configuration interaction, demonstrating accurate potential energy curves for lithium hydride even at avoided crossings with significant charge transfer.
