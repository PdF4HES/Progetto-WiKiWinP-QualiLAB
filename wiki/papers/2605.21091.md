# Large-space and Large-time Asymptotics for the Focusing Nonlinear Schrödinger Soliton Gas

**Autori:** Dedi Yan, Xianguo Geng, Wei Jiao · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21091) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21091)

## Abstract

Large-space and large-time asymptotic behavior of soliton gas for the focusing nonlinear Schrödinger equation is investigated. Constructed as continuum limit of N-soliton solutions with discrete spectrum in two segments, the framework avoids restriction to the imaginary axis. Nonlinear steepest descent combined with g-function mechanisms shows asymptotic description by finite-gap elliptic solutions. Large-time analysis reveals distinct behaviors across regions including exponentially decaying, modulated, and unmodulated elliptic-wave zones.
