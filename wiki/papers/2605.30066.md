# Quasi-disjointness in topological dynamics

**Autori:** Hui Xu, Xiangdong Ye · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30066) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30066)

## Abstract

Introduces quasi-disjointness for minimal dynamical systems, providing equivalent characterizations and proving preservation under factors and extensions. Demonstrates PI and AI systems exhibit quasi-disjointness properties.
