# Dynamic Shapley Computation

**Autori:** Xuan Yang et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20620) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20620)

## Abstract

Introduces D-Shap framework for efficient dynamic data valuation using Shapley values. Exploits utility and coalition locality to enable task updates in milliseconds while reducing player update costs by three orders of magnitude.
