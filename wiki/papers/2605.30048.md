**Autori:** Nik Weaver · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30048) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30048)

## Abstract

Reviews linear and matrix generalizations of Hall's marriage theorem and König's theorem, relating them to generalizations of Dilworth's and Menger's theorems.
