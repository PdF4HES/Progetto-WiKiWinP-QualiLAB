# Low-Overhead Receiver Design for Data-Dependent Superimposed Training via Deep Learning

**Autori:** Xinjie Li, Xingyu Zhou, Jing Zhang et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29995) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29995)

## Abstract

This work proposes a low-overhead transmission framework for data-dependent superimposed training with enhanced interference mitigation. An enhanced receiver achieves non-iterative pilot-data decoupling for quasi-static channels. A Vision Transformer-based neural receiver captures orthogonal structures and channel correlations.
