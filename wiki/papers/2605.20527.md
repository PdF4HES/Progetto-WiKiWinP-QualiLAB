# L^2-Stability for STFT phase retrieval

**Autori:** Susanna Bertolini et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20527) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20527)

## Abstract

The short-time Fourier transform with Gaussian window performs L²-local stable phase retrieval at the constant function. The proof involved collaboration between mathematicians and LLMs. An autoformalization in Lean 4 extends the result to L²-local stable phase retrieval for all Hermite windows and finite spans of canonical basis vectors.
