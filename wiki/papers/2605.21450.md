# Composite B-Spline Current Deposition and Interpolation Operators for Thin-Wire FDTD Simulations

**Autori:** Cole Gruninger, Boyce E. Griffith · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21450) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21450)

## Abstract

Holland-Simpson thin-wire FDTD simulations of obliquely oriented closed-loop antennas exhibit persistent low-frequency parasitic currents. The paper introduces composite B-spline regularizations that satisfy charge conservation requirements to machine precision, with applications to antenna impedance calculations.
