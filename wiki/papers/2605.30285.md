# On the equivariant KU_G local sphere for finite abelian groups

**Autori:** Yingxin Li · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30285) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30285)

## Abstract

This work establishes that the KU_G/p-local sphere spectrum equals homotopy fixed points of a p-complete module spectrum, generalizing prior computations for finite p-groups. The researchers determine both Z-graded and RO(G)-graded homotopy Mackey functors and relate the spectrum to wedge sums of equivariant Morava K-theory localized sphere spectra.
