# Corner Quantization of 4D BF Theory

**Autori:** Giovanni Canepa, Alberto S. Cattaneo, Filippo Fila-Robattino et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30006) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30006)

## Abstract

Examines quantized corner structures in four-dimensional BF theory, classifying corner algebras and constructing representations. In abelian cases, explicit presentations of the corner algebras identify them as infinite-dimensional oscillator-type Lie algebras.
