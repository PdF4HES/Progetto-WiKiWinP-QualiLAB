# Combinatorial manifolds and Kleene's theorem, homotopically

**Autori:** Yorgo Chamoun · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21142) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21142)

## Abstract

A general method to build categories of combinatorial manifolds as coreflective subcategories of categories of relational presheaves, relying crucially on unique factorization systems. The technique is interpreted as building a model category whose cofibrant objects are exactly the combinatorial manifolds. Applications include a category of euclidean precubical sets and an abstract proof of Kleene's theorem from automata theory via manifold automata.
