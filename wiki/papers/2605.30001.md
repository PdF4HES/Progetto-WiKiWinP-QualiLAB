# The Rise of the Software-Defined Vehicle: Architectures, Enabling Technologies, and Future Opportunities

**Autori:** Eirini Liotou, Dimitra Tzelalidou, Gerasimos Christodoulou · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30001) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30001)

## Abstract

This comprehensive survey examines software-defined vehicle architectures, enabling technologies, and operational frameworks. It reviews evolution from distributed electronic control unit systems to domain-based and centralized computing platforms.
