# Structural Infinite-Exponent Partition Relations and Weak Choice Principles

**Autori:** Lyra A. Gardiner, Jonathan Schilhan · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21259) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21259)

## Abstract

Infinite-exponent partition relations on arbitrary relational structures are investigated, focusing on linear orders and graphs. Results show certain relations consistent with ZF imply failure of the Axiom of Choice and related selection principles.
