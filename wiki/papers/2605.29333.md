# Active phase-space topology unifies depletion and alignment in bacterial flows

**Autori:** Mingyang Guan, Bowen Ling, Enhao Liu et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29333) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29333)

## Abstract

The researchers developed an analytical hydrodynamic model to understand how bacteria move in flows at small scales. Their work reveals that shear-induced depletion and alignment are dual manifestations of a single active phase-space topology, offering a unified framework for bacterial transport that goes beyond classical equilibrium theory.
