# A simple model of co-emergence of grid and place fields

**Autori:** Zhaoze Wang et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21356) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21356)

## Abstract

A unified recurrent network model instantiating Dale's Law is trained to predict next sensory observations from masked previous observations and egocentric motion. Grid and place cells co-emerge without supervision, reproducing experimentally observed phenomena including grid fragmentation, merging, and developmental ordering.
