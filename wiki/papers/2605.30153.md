# Diffusion Models Are Statistically Optimal for Learning Low-Dimensional Multi-Modal Distributions

**Autori:** Jingda Wu, Changxiao Cai · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30153) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30153)

## Abstract

We study sample complexity of diffusion models for learning distributions supported on unions of low-dimensional subspaces. For subgaussian data distributions, diffusion models require near-optimal sample complexity depending only on intrinsic dimension, significantly improving prior guarantees. Results apply without smoothness, bounded-density, or log-concavity assumptions.
