# Set Shaping Theory as a Complementary Payload-Shaping Layer for Steganography

**Autori:** Aida Koch et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19885) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19885)

## Abstract

Explores Set Shaping Theory as preprocessing for LSB image steganography, reducing statistical detectability. Testing showed average of 25.16% relative reduction in KL divergence with controlled simulations.
