# Invariant statistical connections on the multivariate centered Gaussian model and their moduli spaces

**Autori:** Hideyuki Ishi, Hikozo Kobayashi, Takayuki Okuda · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30300) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30300)

## Abstract

Research on invariant statistical connections within zero-mean multivariate normal distributions equipped with the Fisher metric, introducing moduli spaces via categorical equivalence relations and determining GL(n,ℝ)-invariant connections.
