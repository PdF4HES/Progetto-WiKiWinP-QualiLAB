# On L^p-spaces of functions with values in locally convex spaces

**Autori:** Matthieu F. Pinaud, Humberto Prado · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30191) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30191)

## Abstract

Study of Lusin-measurable functions in locally convex spaces, examining pointwise limits and establishing approximation results for L^p-spaces with density theorems for simple functions.
