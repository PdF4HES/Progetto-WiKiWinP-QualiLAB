# The Topological Stability Index: A Variance-Based Measure for Persistence Barcodes

**Autori:** Joris Kirchner, Ioannis Diamantis · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29839) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29839)

## Abstract

A variance-based scalar measure for persistence barcodes that quantifies the dispersion of persistence lifetimes, introducing the Topological Stability Index (TSI) and its normalized variant with connections to Rényi entropy.
