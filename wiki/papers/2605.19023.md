# First-passage processes in a deterministic one-dimensional cellular automaton model of traffic flow

**Autori:** Ofer Biham, Gilad Hertzberg Rabinovich, Eytan Katzav · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19023) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19023)

## Abstract

Analytical results for first-passage processes in a deterministic one-dimensional cellular automaton model of traffic flow are presented. Starting from random initial states with car density p, cars move right if the adjacent cell is empty. The model exhibits a continuous dynamical phase transition at p=1/2 between free-flowing and congested phases. Closed-form expressions are derived for first-stopping time distributions, stopping probabilities, and last-stopping time distributions in the low-density phase.
