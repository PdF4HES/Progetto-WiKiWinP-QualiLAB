# An Exponential Sample-Complexity Advantage for Coherent Quantum Inference

**Autori:** Zhaoyi Li et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21457) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21457)

## Abstract

Standard quantum inference converts quantum data into classical outputs. This work examines an alternative setting where output remains quantum, preserving coherence. Coherent protocols achieve exponentially lower sample complexity than measurement-mediated incoherent approaches. For quantum purity amplification with d-dimensional inputs, coherent processing reaches error ε using O(1/ε) copies versus Ω(d/ε) copies for incoherent protocols.
