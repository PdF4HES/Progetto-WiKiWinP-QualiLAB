# On the origin of the Gibbons conjecture

**Autori:** Renan J. S. Isneri · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29848) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29848)

## Abstract

The term Gibbons conjecture is widely used in connection with symmetry results for the Allen-Cahn equation. However, its origin is less transparent. The note traces the term's emergence to a 1995 Carbou paper and explores how mathematical terminology develops through informal communication.
