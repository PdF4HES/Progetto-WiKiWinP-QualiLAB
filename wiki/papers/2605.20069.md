# Smooth Partial Lotteries for Stable Randomized Selection

**Autori:** Alexander Goldberg, Giulia Fanti, Nihar B. Shah · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20069) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20069)

## Abstract

Proposes smoothness principle for partial lottery designs in competitive selection. Introduces Clipped Linear Lottery mechanism achieving optimal smoothness-regret tradeoffs, validated on peer review data from ICLR and NeurIPS.
