# Flow loops and quantum groups

**Autori:** Sunghyuk Park · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21382) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21382)

## Abstract

This paper connects quantum group invariants and Morse flow dynamics for studying knots. For fibered knots, a two-variable series invariant is defined by counting Morse flow loops in the complement. This dynamical series is conjectured to agree with the BPS q-series of the knot complement, which arises from Verma modules for quantum groups and encodes all colored Jones polynomials. The correspondence is proven for all braid-homogeneous knots.
