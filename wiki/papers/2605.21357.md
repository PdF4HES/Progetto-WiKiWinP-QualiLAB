# Physical completion of the Navier-Stokes equations

**Autori:** Samuel L. Braunstein · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21357) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21357)

## Abstract

The incompressible Navier-Stokes equations lack thermal noise. Using topological arguments based on Poincaré's lemma, this work derives the fluctuation-dissipation relation for nonlinear dynamics without linearisation assumptions. The resulting stochastic system with molecular-scale spectral cutoff is globally well-posed and converges exponentially to a unique Gibbs equilibrium.
