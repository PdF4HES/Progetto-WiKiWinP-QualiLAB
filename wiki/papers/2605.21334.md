# RSE of a Quantum Transport Code and its Effects

**Autori:** Christoph Conrads, Edoardo Di Napoli · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21334) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21334)

## Abstract

Research software engineering experiences over two years with libNEGF, a quantum transport code. Systematic application of continuous integration, automated testing, and continuous benchmarking revealed critical defects: uninitialized memory reads, out-of-bounds writes, and a misunderstood mathematical model in boundary condition handling. Continuous benchmarking also exposed performance regressions caused by HPC system configuration changes.
