# Where not to find the spectrum of the partial theta function

**Autori:** Yousra Gati, Vladimir Petrov Kostov · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29903) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29903)

## Abstract

Demonstrates spectrum-free regions for Ramanujan's partial theta function, establishing that no spectral value exists in specified sectors and disks within the unit disk.
