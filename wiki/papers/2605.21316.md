# Bitcoin's Power Law: Weak Structure, Strong Forecasts

**Autori:** Carlos Baquero, Raquel Menezes · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21316) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21316)

## Abstract

Tests Bitcoin price power law claims using the Clauset-Shalizi-Newman protocol and time-domain adaptations. Finds that distributional power law is rejected on several metrics, but the simple power law dominates 12–24 month forecasting horizons against standard baselines.
