# Solitonic Construction of Artificial Neural Networks from Nonlinear Field Theory

**Autori:** R. A. C. Correa · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27568) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27568)

## Abstract

The paper presents a field-theoretic construction of a class of artificial neural networks from solitonic degrees of freedom in nonlinear scalar field theory. The approach derives neural layers from collective-coordinate reductions of localized field configurations rather than treating them as simple renaming of standard layers. For the φ⁴ kink model, the authors show how tanh activation and logistic sigmoid functions arise from kink profiles, with multilayer feedforward architecture derived from operator-splitting approximations.
