# x-periodic Quasi One Dimensional Anomalous (Rogue) Waves in Multidimensional Nonlinear Schrödinger Equations: Fission, Fusion, and Recurrence

**Autori:** Francesco Coppini, Paolo Maria Santini · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26961) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.26961)

## Abstract

The authors examine x-periodic anomalous (rogue, freak, extreme) waves in multidimensional nonlinear Schrödinger equations, focusing on modulation instability dynamics. They demonstrate that the initial nonlinear stage is universal, independent of the particular MNLS model, described through perturbations of the Akhmediev breather solution. The work analyzes recurrence patterns showing significant differences among various equation types, with successive stages exhibiting progressively intricate fission and fusion processes. Using finite gap perturbation theory, they provide quantitative predictions validated against numerical simulations, suggesting observable phenomena across water waves, optics, plasma physics, and condensed matter systems.
