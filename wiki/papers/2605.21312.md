# Frontier: Towards Comprehensive and Accurate LLM Inference Simulation

**Autori:** Yicheng Feng et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21312) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21312)

## Abstract

Introduces a discrete-event simulator for modern LLM inference serving with disaggregated abstractions. Captures co-location, prefill-decode disaggregation, and runtime optimizations, achieving average throughput error below 4% and reducing latency error from 44.9% to 6.4%.
