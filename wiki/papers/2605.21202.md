# Asymptotic analysis for approximate harmonic maps from degenerating cylinders and applications to minimal surfaces

**Autori:** Jiayu Li, Lei Liu, Miaomiao Zhu · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21202) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21202)

## Abstract

Blow-up analysis and quantitative behavior are investigated for sequences of maps from degenerating tori or cylinders with free boundary conditions to compact Riemannian manifolds. Generalized energy identities are established, and asymptotic limits of necks are characterized as geodesics or geodesic-like curves, with applications to minimal cylinders with free boundary.
