# A Nash Equilibrium Framework For Training-Free Multimodal Step Verification

**Autori:** Rohit Sinha et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20033) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20033)

## Abstract

Proposes training-free verification approach treating step-wise verification as coordination game. Achieves improvements of 2.4% to 5.2% over baseline models using disagreement-aware filtering and equilibrium scoring.
