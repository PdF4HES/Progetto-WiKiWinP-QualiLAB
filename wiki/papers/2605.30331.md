# Majorization precursors to supermodularity and subadditivity on the majorization lattice

**Autori:** Alexander Stévins, Michael G. Jabbour, Serge Deside et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30331) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30331)

## Abstract

We establish two structural majorization relations underlying properties of supermodularity and subadditivity on the lattice induced by majorization. We show supermodularity and subadditivity of Tsallis and Rényi entropies, recovering these properties for Shannon entropy. We further strengthen these inequalities, demonstrating strict subadditivity and strict supermodularity on the majorization lattice.
