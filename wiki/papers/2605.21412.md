# A biquaternionic reformulation of Maxwell's equations via Fourier analysis

**Autori:** Aarón Guillén-Villalobos et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21412) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21412)

## Abstract

This work analyzes the parabolic Dirac operator in a biquaternionic setting, characterizing its kernel through generalized div-curl systems. Using Fourier transforms, the authors characterize fundamental solution transforms and construct a right inverse operator. The approach yields explicit vectorial solutions to time-dependent Maxwell systems.
