# A categorification of Kauffman states for planar graphs

**Autori:** Giovanni Cerulli Irelli et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19872) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19872)

## Abstract

Given a decorated planar graph (G, ω), angular functions ω-compatible are studied and two directed graphs — L(G,ω) and BMS(G,ω) — are analyzed. Sufficient conditions for L(G,ω) to be a graded distributive lattice are given, recovering Kauffman's Clock Theorem when G is a knot diagram. A representation of the corresponding quiver with potential is associated to every BMS state, yielding an isomorphism generalizing a result of Bazier-Matte-Schiffler.
