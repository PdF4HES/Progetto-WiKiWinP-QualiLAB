# Classification and Isotropy of σ-Derivations on the Quantum Plane

**Autori:** R. Baltazar, R. Cavalheiro · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20516) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20516)

## Abstract

Classification of sigma-derivations on the quantum plane and their isotropy groups under automorphism conjugation. Analysis of both general and singular cases where q equals minus one, with explicit character equation descriptions.
