# Matching A with F in long-range QFTs

**Autori:** Lorenzo Benfatto, Omar Zanusso · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21326) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21326)

## Abstract

This paper studies renormalisation group flow in long-range multiscalar theories, showing that the flow satisfies gradient structure up to third loop order. Results demonstrate that the A-function and metric can be matched with sphere free-energy and Zamolodchikov's metric, providing a perturbative proof of the F-theorem.
