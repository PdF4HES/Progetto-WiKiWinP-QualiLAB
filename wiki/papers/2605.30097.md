# Action accessibility in the variety of skew braces

**Autori:** Andrea Albano, Paola Stefanelli · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30097) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30097)

## Abstract

Addresses a question from the 2025 Oberwolfach Mini-Workshop by demonstrating that the variety of skew braces is not action accessible by investigating the categorical notion of centraliser in the sense of Huq.
