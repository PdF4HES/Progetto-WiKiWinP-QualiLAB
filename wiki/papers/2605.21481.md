# AiraXiv: An AI-Driven Open-Access Platform for Human and AI Scientists

**Autori:** Junshu Pan et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21481) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21481)

## Abstract

AiraXiv is an AI-driven open-access platform built on open preprints, AI-augmented analysis and review, and reader feedback. It supports human scientists through an interactive UI and AI scientists through MCP-based interactions. Validated through real-world deployments including serving as the submission platform for ICAIS 2025.
