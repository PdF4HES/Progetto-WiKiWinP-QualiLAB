# From Coefficients to Distributions: De Moivre and the Operational View of Probability

**Autori:** R. Labouriau · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.25227) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.25227)

## Abstract

Traces a conceptual genealogy from Abraham de Moivre's derivation of the normal curve (1733) to the modern distributional approach to statistics, formulating a distributional version of the De Moivre-Laplace theorem.
