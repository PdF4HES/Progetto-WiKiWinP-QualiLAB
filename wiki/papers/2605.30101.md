# List Recovery for Random Low-Rate Linear Codes

**Autori:** Isaac M Hair, Amit Sahai · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30101) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30101)

## Abstract

We prove list recovery guarantees for random low-rate linear codes over sufficiently large prime fields. Using graph-theoretic certificates and the Schwartz-Zippel lemma, we establish results for fixed dimension and error fraction. We also provide lower bounds showing limitations for feasible list sizes.
