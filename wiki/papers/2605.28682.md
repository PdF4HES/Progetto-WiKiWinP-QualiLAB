**Autori:** Élie Casbi · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28682) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.28682)

## Abstract

Constructs a monoidal category for acyclic quivers and establishes the existence of a family of complete exceptional sequences in mod kQ satisfying many good properties.
