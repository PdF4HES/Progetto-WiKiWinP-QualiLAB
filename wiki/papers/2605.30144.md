# AgentSchool: An LLM-Powered Multi-Agent Simulation for Education

**Autori:** Yulei Ye, Wenhao Li, Zhong Wen et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30144) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30144)

## Abstract

AgentSchool is an LLM-driven multi-agent simulator modeling learning as state transition rather than prompted behavior. Cognitively growable student agents are equipped with weighted subject knowledge graphs, thinking-workflow pools, and explicit misconceptions, coupled with adaptive teacher agents that plan and scaffold along the Zone of Proximal Development. A configurable scenery generator situates instruction within formal and informal learning fields, producing differentiated mastery and misconception traces.
