# Deep Tech to Space: Space Data Centers and AI Revolution at the Edge

**Autori:** Jonas Weiss et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19892) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19892)

## Abstract

Addresses challenges of space-generated data volume through software-driven, multi-tenant AI platforms capable of processing data in orbit. Analyzes Low Earth Orbit satellite constellation architecture and demonstrates feasibility through Earth observation use cases.
