# Ergodic Theorems, Almost Invariant Sets, and Values of Averages

**Autori:** Valery V. Ryzhikov · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30309) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30309)

## Abstract

Provides proofs of ergodic theorems by von Neumann, Birkhoff, Wiener, and Rokhlin's lemma for Z^d-actions. Examines deviations of ergodic averages that tend to zero almost everywhere and their distribution properties under scale changes.
