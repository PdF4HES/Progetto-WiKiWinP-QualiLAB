# Supercooling of liquids, as described by the Enskog-Vlasov kinetic equation

**Autori:** E. S. Benilov · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30084) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30084)

## Abstract

Application of Enskog-Vlasov kinetic model to supercooling phenomena. Analysis shows spinodal temperature calculation and divergence of surface tension at this critical point, with oscillatory regions emerging as instability approaches.
