# Constrained integrability and anyonic chains

**Autori:** Matthew Blakeney, Luke Corcoran, Marius de Leeuw · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28946) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.28946)

## Abstract

The paper reviews Yang-Baxter integrability for constrained spin chains, focusing on anyonic chains derived from fusion category constraints. Key contributions include discussing Temperley-Lieb algebra emergence, extending integrable anyonic chain results across fusion categories up to rank 7, and discovering new integrable models. These include spin-3/2 models for su(2)_k fusion categories and chains based on Tambara-Yamagami and product fusion categories, plus findings on Haagerup-Izumi fusion category systems.
