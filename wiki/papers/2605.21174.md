# Exact expression for maximum Lyapunov exponent during transients in computationally powerful dynamical networks

**Autori:** Arthur S. Powanwe et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21174) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21174)

## Abstract

Study of network dynamics with exact solvability through nonlinear coordinate transformation. Derives analytical expression for time-dependent maximum Lyapunov exponent, demonstrating positive MLEs during transients useful for computation. Framework enables algebraic control of transient lifetimes through network connectivity and initial conditions.
