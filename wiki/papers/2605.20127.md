# Beyond Prediction Accuracy: Target-Space Recovery Profiles for Evaluating Model-Brain Alignment

**Autori:** Ken Nakamura et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20127) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20127)

## Abstract

A framework evaluates model-brain alignment by identifying which response dimensions are recovered during prediction. Results show that prediction accuracy alone can mask mismatches, as pretrained and random models sometimes achieve comparable scores with distinct recovery profiles.
