# Smart strategies to navigate turbulent odor plumes reorienting to local wind

**Autori:** Lorenzo Piro et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21329) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21329)

## Abstract

Olfactory search in turbulent environments presents a sensorimotor challenge. This work presents a wind-relative reinforcement-learning framework where agents navigate turbulent plumes using elapsed time since last odor detection and locally estimated wind direction. Policies trained in direct numerical simulations outperform cast-and-surge strategies and reveal temporal wind integration as regime-dependent.
