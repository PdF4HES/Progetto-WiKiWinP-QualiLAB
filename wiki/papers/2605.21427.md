# PALS: Power-Aware LLM Serving for Mixture-of-Experts Models

**Autori:** Can Hankendi et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21427) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21427)

## Abstract

A power-aware runtime for LLM serving that treats GPU power caps as a controllable resource, jointly optimizing them with software parameters. The system combines offline power-performance models with feedback-driven control to satisfy throughput targets while maximizing energy efficiency, demonstrating improvements up to 26.3% in energy efficiency.
