# The generalized method of moments is (almost) statistically efficient in low-SNR Gaussian latent-variable models

**Autori:** Amnon Balanov, Tamir Bendory, Dan Edidin · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30095) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30095)

## Abstract

We study estimation in low signal-to-noise ratio regimes for Gaussian latent-variable models. The generalized method-of-moments matches maximum likelihood's asymptotic efficiency when moment features are optimally chosen and weighted. Analysis reveals layered local geometry where different directions become informative at different moment orders.
