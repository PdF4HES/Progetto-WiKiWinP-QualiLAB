**Autori:** Dongsung Huh · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29885) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29885)

## Abstract

Proposes Cayley-table completion as testbed for discovering discrete algorithmic biases in deep learning. Challenges community to establish formal recovery bounds and generalize continuous priors to autonomously discover discrete algorithmic axioms.
