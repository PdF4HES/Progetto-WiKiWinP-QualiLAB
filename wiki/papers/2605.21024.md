# Smooth categories in a 6 functor formalism and compact generation for nuclear categories in analytic geometry

**Autori:** Matteo Montagnani · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21024) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21024)

## Abstract

The notion of smooth ∞-categories is studied within the framework of a six-functor formalism. Using condensed mathematics and analytic stacks theory, a rigid analytic variety is shown to be smooth if and only if its associated category of nuclear sheaves is smooth. Furthermore, compact generation of the nuclear sheaves category is related to rigidity algebraization, obtaining an example of a non-atomically generated but internally smooth category.
