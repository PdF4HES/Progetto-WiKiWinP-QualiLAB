# Exponential Lower Bounds for the Pfaffian Number of Graphs

**Autori:** Priyanshu Pant, Ranveer Singh · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21077) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21077)

## Abstract

The authors demonstrate exponentially many Pfaffians are necessary for representing perfect-matching polynomials of graphs on orientable surfaces, establishing that the maximum Pfaffian number is at least (8/3)^g for genus g graphs, improving prior linear bounds for complete bipartite graphs.
