**Autori:** Xuan Gong, Dekui Peng · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.25445) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.25445)

## Abstract

Examines local topological structure through Tukey order, introducing the fineness index for directed posets and establishing character bounds for topological groups with specific base properties.
