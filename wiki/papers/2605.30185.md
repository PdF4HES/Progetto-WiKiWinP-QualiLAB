# Theory of distribution skewness effect on polydisperse random close packing

**Autori:** Vinay Vaibhav, Carmine Anzivino, Alessio Zaccone · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30185) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30185)

## Abstract

Investigation of random close packing density in polydisperse hard sphere systems using equilibrium crowding model. Derives closed-form solution for packing density in terms of diameter distribution moments, exploring effects of polydispersity and skewness. Predictions are validated against numerical results.
