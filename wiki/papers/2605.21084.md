# Label-free SERS Discrimination of Native Proline Hydroxylation at Single-molecule peptide by Deep Learning-assisted plasmonic nanopore

**Autori:** Yingqi Zhao et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21084) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21084)

## Abstract

A particle-in-pore SERS platform combined with peak occurrence frequency analysis and 1D-CNN discriminates hydroxylated peptide fragments. CNN-based classification achieved accuracies up to 89.74%, with gradient-weighted visualization confirming chemical relevance of learned patterns for detecting weak post-translational modifications.
