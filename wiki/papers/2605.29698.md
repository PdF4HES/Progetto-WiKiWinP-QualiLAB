# A Systematic Evaluation of Molecular Mixture Behavior Prediction

**Autori:** Roel J. Leenhouts, Nathan K. Morgan, William Green et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29698) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29698)

## Abstract

This research proposes an evaluation framework that decomposes mixture-property error into pure-compound and interaction (non-ideal) components, revealing that strong accuracy can mask poor recovery of non-ideal behavior.
