# Modeling and Resource Optimization for Quantum Oracles

**Autori:** Zhihang Li et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21380) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21380)

## Abstract

Quantum oracles are prevalent in many quantum algorithms, with resource consumption directly affecting performance. A Hierarchical Recursive Synthesis-Evaluation (HRSE) model is introduced enabling formal oracle description and precise gate complexity analysis. An Adaptive Space-depth Trade-off (ASDT) algorithm generates oracle structures under fixed qubit constraints. Results show 53.99% average circuit depth reduction versus W-cycle approaches.
