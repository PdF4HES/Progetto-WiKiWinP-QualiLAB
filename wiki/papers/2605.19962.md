# Computing the Arc-Deletion Distance to Orchard Networks is NP-hard

**Autori:** Peng Li, Zhiwei Liu, Yangjing Long · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19962) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19962)

## Abstract

This research proves that computing arc-deletion distance to orchard networks is NP-hard through reduction from Degree-3 Vertex Cover, establishing computational intractability of this phylogenetic network transformation measure.
