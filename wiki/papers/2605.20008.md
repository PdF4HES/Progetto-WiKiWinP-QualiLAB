# Central idempotents in group-graded rings

**Autori:** Johan Öinert · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20008) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20008)

## Abstract

Investigation of central idempotents in G-graded rings, establishing finite support groups under abelian or non-annihilation conditions. Extends Bass and Burns results to non-commutative rings with applications to Leavitt path rings and related structures.
