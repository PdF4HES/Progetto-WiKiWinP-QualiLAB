# Introduction to Higher Order Classical Dynamics: Pais-Uhlenbeck Model and Coupled Oscillators

**Autori:** Cássius Anderson Miquele de Melo, Ivan Francisco de Souza · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20094) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20094)

## Abstract

Most laws of Nature involve derivatives up to the second order. Ostrogradski extended Hamilton's equations by considering Lagrangians depending on higher-order derivatives of generalized coordinates. This work demonstrates how the Hamilton-Ostrogradski formalism applies to the Pais-Uhlenbeck oscillator, intended as a basis for discussion in advanced classical mechanics courses.
