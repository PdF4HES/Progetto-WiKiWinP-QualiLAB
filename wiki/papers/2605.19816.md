# Performance of low vision individuals when selecting a target with head-pointing in virtual reality

**Autori:** Camille Bordeau et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19816) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19816)

## Abstract

Low vision patients with central visual field loss performed visually-guided pointing tasks in VR using head-contingent cursors. Results show patients require larger pointer activation zones than controls but achieve comparable performance, with implications for accessible interfaces.
