# Automated Discovery of Metainterfaces with Tailored Friction Laws

**Autori:** Li Fu et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19555) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19555)

## Abstract

A numerical optimization-based inverse design framework for automatically discovering metainterfaces that satisfy specified relationships between friction and normal forces. The framework expands achievable friction coefficients, unlocks power-law friction laws with exponents between 2/3 and 1.35, and achieves bilinear laws. Relevant cases are validated experimentally, enabling systematic exploration of parameter spaces.
