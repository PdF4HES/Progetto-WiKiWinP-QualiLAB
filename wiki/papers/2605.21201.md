# Relative trace formulas for obstacle scattering with Neumann and transmission boundary conditions

**Autori:** Arne Hofmann, Alexander Strohmaier · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21201) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21201)

## Abstract

Relative trace formulas for scattering by multiple obstacles are established under Neumann and transmission boundary conditions. For f(x) = x^{1/2}, the trace represents Casimir energy. One-dimensional analysis recovers a rigorous Lifshitz formula for parallel plates with frequency-independent permittivity and permeability.
