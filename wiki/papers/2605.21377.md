**Autori:** Robbe Brants · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21377) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21377)

## Abstract

Demonstrates that standalone hydrodynamic effective field theories are inherently acausal and require transient ultraviolet modes for causality restoration while maintaining late-timescale hydrodynamic behavior.
