# Differential graded Hopf algebra structure on free symmetric cosimplicial operads

**Autori:** Calvin Tcheka, Batkam Mbatchou V. Jacky, Guy R. Biyogmam · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29878) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29878)

## Abstract

The authors construct new chain complex algebras and bicomplex structures on free symmetric connected multiplicative differential graded operads. They establish a differential graded Hopf algebra structure using the odot product and an Alexander-Whitney homomorphism analogue, extending the Malvenuto-Reutenauer result.
