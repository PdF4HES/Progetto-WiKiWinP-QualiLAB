# Quantifying Hyperparameter Transfer and the Importance of Embedding Layer Learning Rate

**Autori:** Dayal Singh Kalra, Maissam Barkeshli · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21486) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21486)

## Abstract

A framework to quantify hyperparameter transfer through three metrics: quality of the scaling law fit, robustness to extrapolation errors, and asymptotic loss penalty due to parameterization choice. The overwhelming benefit of μP relative to SP when training with AdamW arises simply from maximizing the learning rate of the embedding layer. Increasing it by a factor of width to match μP dramatically smooths training while improving hyperparameter transfer.
