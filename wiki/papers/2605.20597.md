# Atomic Characterization and Its Applications of Matrix-Weighted Variable Hardy Spaces

**Autori:** Yiqun Chen et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20597) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20597)

## Abstract

Variable Hardy space H^{p(·)}_W on ℝⁿ with matrix weight W and variable exponent p(·) having globally log-Hölder continuity is introduced via matrix-weighted grand maximal functions. Atomic characterization is obtained through refined Whitney decomposition combined with convex-body reducing operators. Applications include dual space determination and boundedness of Calderón-Zygmund operators.
