# Arbitrary-genus dark soliton gases in the defocusing nonlinear Schrödinger hydrodynamics

**Autori:** Marco Bertola et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18651) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.18651)

## Abstract

The defocusing nonlinear Schrödinger hydrodynamics supports exact dark solitons under finite density boundary conditions. This work introduces an arbitrary-genus potential of dark soliton gases through the limit of N-dark solitons as N approaches infinity. The dynamics exhibit cascading behaviors across unmodulated and modulated genus-N regions, progressively reducing to planar regions.
