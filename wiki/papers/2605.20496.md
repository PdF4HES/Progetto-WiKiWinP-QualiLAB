# Platonic Representations in the Human Brain: Unsupervised Recovery of Universal Geometry

**Autori:** Pablo Marcos-Manchón, Rishi Jha, Lluís Fuentemilla · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20496) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20496)

## Abstract

A self-supervised encoder learns subject-specific embeddings from fMRI data by exploiting repeated stimulus presentations. Results demonstrate that independently learned brain spaces can be translated across subjects using unsupervised orthogonal rotations, revealing shared neural geometry.
