# Multicategorical Semantics for Untyped Effects

**Autori:** Ariel Grunfeld, Liron Cohen · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21337) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21337)

## Abstract

Completeness proofs in categorical semantics usually proceed by building a syntactic category whose composition is given by substitution. For untyped effectful Call-by-Value languages, this runs into a basic obstacle: there is no canonical notion of simultaneous substitution of computations, since evaluation order is semantically meaningful. This work formalizes the idea in a one-object Freyd-multicategorical setting, introduces Freyd operads, and proves soundness, initiality, and completeness, yielding categorical semantics tailored to untyped effectful computation.
