# A construction of tame sheaves and tame de Rham-Witt cohomology

**Autori:** Alberto Merici, Kay Rülling, Shuji Saito · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20858) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20858)

## Abstract

An algebraic version of the tame site of a pair (X, X̃) providing general machinery to construct a tame sheaf from étale sheaf data and local tame sections. Application to big de Rham-Witt sheaves with tame sections defined by log poles and reciprocity sheaves. Comparison of tame syntomic cohomology with the Nygaard filtration on the tame de Rham-Witt complex.
