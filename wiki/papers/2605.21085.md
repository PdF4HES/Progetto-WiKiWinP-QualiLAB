# Decoupling Communication from Policy: Robust MARL under Bandwidth Constraints

**Autori:** Alexi Canesse et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21085) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21085)

## Abstract

This work addresses multi-agent reinforcement learning under bandwidth constraints by decoupling communication pathways from policy representations. The method, SLIM, introduces a normalized bandwidth budget metric and achieves state-of-the-art performance with robustness under limited communication.
