# Motion-Coupled Sensing: When the State Change Powers Its Own Sensing

**Autori:** Muhammad Tahir et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19793) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19793)

## Abstract

Demonstrates batteryless IoT systems where mechanical motion supplies energy for sensing and transmission. Field deployments achieved 99.3% per-event transmission reliability for waste-bin monitoring across 5,945 actuations.
