# The Curious Case of Max Planck retracted papers

**Autori:** Yves Gingras, Mahdi Khelfaoui · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.17534) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.17534)

## Abstract

Two papers published by Max Planck in Naturwissenschaften were retrospectively marked as retracted on Springer's digital platform. These withdrawals stem from contemporary digitization and copyright-management procedures applied anachronistically to historical publications. Republication across multiple formats was a common and legitimate practice within early 20th century scientific publishing culture. The analysis argues that contemporary notions like duplicate publication and self-plagiarism cannot be applied retrospectively without distorting the historical record.
