# Anisotropic calibrations, adiabatic limits and mirror symmetry

**Autori:** Kotaro Kawai, Tommaso Pacini · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21161) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21161)

## Abstract

A 1-parameter family of differential forms is defined from calibrations and calibrated distributions, with its adiabatic limit analyzed. Applications to G₂-manifolds establish connections between adiabatic calibrated conditions and Fueter-type equations, with explicit examples and analytic existence theorems provided.
