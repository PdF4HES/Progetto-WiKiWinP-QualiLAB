# Rational Weyl group elements of odd type D

**Autori:** Yutong Zhang, Yaoran Yang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20928) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20928)

## Abstract

The paper proves that rational Weyl group elements in type Dr with r odd consist of the longest element and signed cyclic elements indexed by non-empty subsets, confirming and strengthening a conjecture about their count.
