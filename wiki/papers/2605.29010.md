# On the top-degree cohomology groups of congruence subgroups of Sp_{2n}(Z)

**Autori:** Fabio Capovilla-Searle · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29010) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29010)

## Abstract

This paper proves that the top-degree cohomology group surjects onto homology of the symplectic Tits building quotient, computes that quotient's homology, and establishes that this cohomology is nontrivial with a computable rank lower bound.
