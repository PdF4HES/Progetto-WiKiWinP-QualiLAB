# Lumina: An AI-Augmented Multiscale Material Informatics Framework

**Autori:** Pradeep Kumar Seshadri et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21172) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21172)

## Abstract

Lumina is a modular Python-based informatics framework centralizing multiscale material data from atomistic simulations to macro-scale experimental records. It employs hierarchical XML-based schemas and dynamic runtime parsing for schema-independent parameter extraction. The platform provides visualization modules and integrates a conversational AI assistant for intelligent material retrieval and natural language querying.
