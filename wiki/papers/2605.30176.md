# The Fermionic Signature Operator in the Reissner-Nordström Geometry in Horizon-Penetrating Coordinates

**Autori:** Felix Finster, Christoph Krpoun · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30176) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30176)

## Abstract

Study of the Dirac equation in Reissner-Nordström geometry, proving a mass decomposition theorem and demonstrating that fermionic operators are bounded symmetric on the solution space.
