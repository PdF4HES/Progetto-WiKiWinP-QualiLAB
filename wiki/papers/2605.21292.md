# Large-Step Training Dynamics of a Two-Factor Linear Transformer Model

**Autori:** Krishnakumar Balasubramanian · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21292) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21292)

## Abstract

Gradient-flow analyses of simplified linear transformers are extended to study finite-step behavior at large learning rates. The dynamics reduce to a two-factor product map with effective step-size parameter μ. Results show that large constant learning rates can change the training attractor rather than merely accelerating convergence.
