# SAOITHE: Sustainable Age-of-Information-Based Timely Status Updating for Hardware-constrained Edge networks

**Autori:** Shih-Kai Chou et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21328) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21328)

## Abstract

Carbon-aware status updating formulated as constrained MDP minimizing AoI subject to carbon footprint budget constraints. Proposes Whittle-index scheduling for scalable real-time processing in edge networks.
