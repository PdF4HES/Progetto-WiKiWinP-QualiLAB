# VideoMLA: Low-Rank Latent KV Cache for Minute-Scale Autoregressive Video Diffusion

**Autori:** Hidir Yesiltepe, Jiazhen Hu, Tuna Han Salih Meral et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30351) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30351)

## Abstract

Introduces VideoMLA, which replaces per-head keys and values with shared low-rank content latent and decoupled 3D-RoPE positional key, reducing per-token KV memory by 92.7%. Investigates why Multi-Head Latent Attention succeeds in video diffusion despite pretrained attention lacking low-rank properties.
