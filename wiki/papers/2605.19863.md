# E-theory of X-C*-algebras and functor formalisms

**Autori:** Ulrich Bunke · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19863) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19863)

## Abstract

Demonstrates E-theory for locally compact Hausdorff spaces constitutes a six-functor formalism equivalent to E-valued sheaves. Shows equivalence for locale E-theory categories to E-valued cosheaves.
