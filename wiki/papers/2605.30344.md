# Tiny but Trusted: Efficient Vision-Language Reasoning for Time-Series Anomaly Detection

**Autori:** Xiaona Zhou, Muntasir Wahed, Tianjiao Yu et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30344) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30344)

## Abstract

Constructs VisAnomBench, a benchmark augmented with high-quality anomaly explanations. Develops VisAnomReasoner, a parameter-efficient VLM for time-series anomaly detection achieving significant improvements in precision and F1 metrics.
