# You Only Need Minimal RLVR Training: Extrapolating LLMs via Rank-1 Trajectories

**Autori:** Zhepei Wei et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21468) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21468)

## Abstract

Demonstrates that reinforcement learning with verifiable rewards trajectories exhibit low-rank structure. Proposes RELEX method for efficient LLM improvement, requiring minimal training steps while predicting future checkpoints through linear extrapolation.
