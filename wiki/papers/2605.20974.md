# Finite factorization is detected by undermonoids

**Autori:** Yutong Zhang, Yaoran Yang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20974) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20974)

## Abstract

The paper addresses whether finite factorization property is hereditary by showing that a cancellative commutative monoid has all submonoids with finite factorization if and only if all undermonoids do.
