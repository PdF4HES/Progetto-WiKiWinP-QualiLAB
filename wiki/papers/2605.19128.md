# A Scaling-Parameter Framework for Perimeter and Area in Self-Similar Planar Fractals

**Autori:** Pedro Marotta · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19128) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19128)

## Abstract

Develops a unified parameter-space representation for self-similar planar constructions organized by two integers: the number of self-similar pieces N and the inverse linear scale factor r. The framework partitions the (N,r) parameter space into three regimes corresponding to qualitatively distinct asymptotic behaviors of perimeter and area. Within the intermediate regime, a construction-class refinement distinguishes additive from subtractive constructions.
