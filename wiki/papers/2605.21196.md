# Effect of grid anisotropy, resolution, and subgrid-scale models in pseudo-spectral LES of low-level clouds

**Autori:** Davide Selvatici, Richard J. A. M. Stevens · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21196) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21196)

## Abstract

Investigation of grid resolution and subgrid-scale model effects on low-level cloud simulations using pseudo-spectral advection with anisotropic minimum dissipation. Analysis of DYCOMS-II and ASTEX campaigns identifies optimal grid anisotropy with vertical spacing approximately three times finer than horizontal spacing.
