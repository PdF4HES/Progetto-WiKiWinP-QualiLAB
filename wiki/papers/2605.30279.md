**Autori:** Siqing Zhang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30279) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30279)

## Abstract

The paper examines crystalline differential operators on smooth varieties and their relationship to Azumaya algebras, demonstrating conditions under which the Azumaya algebra does not split on certain covers.
