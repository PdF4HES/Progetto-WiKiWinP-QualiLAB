# Lower bounds for the low Steklov eigenvalues

**Autori:** Tirumala Chakradhar, Bruno Colbois, Asma Hassannezhad · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30254) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30254)

## Abstract

Establishes geometric lower bounds for low Steklov eigenvalues on compact Riemannian manifolds using trace inequalities relating eigenvalues to Neumann problems and electrical resistance interpretations.
