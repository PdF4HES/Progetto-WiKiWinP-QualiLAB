# On hyperbolic corners and unit-area triangles in planar sets of large measure

**Autori:** Aleksandar Bulj, Vjekoslav Kovač · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30033) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30033)

## Abstract

Research on measurable sets avoiding specific point configurations forming axis-aligned right triangles. The work establishes upper bounds on set measures and makes progress on an Erdős conjecture regarding triangles of fixed area.
