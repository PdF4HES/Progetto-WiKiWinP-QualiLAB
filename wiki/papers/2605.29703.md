# Subcortical Shape Variations and Their Associations with Cognition Across the 8th Decade of Life

**Autori:** Maria del C. Valdes-Hernandez, Wonjung Park, Joanna Moodie et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29703) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29703)

## Abstract

The study explores shape changes in subcortical brain structures across the 8th decade of life using neuroimaging and cognitive data from the Lothian Birth Cohort 1936. Subcortical shape changes were heterogeneous, with the hippocampus and ventral DC experiencing varied morphological deformations, while thalami and globus pallidi showed more uniform volume contraction. Changes in general cognition were mainly associated with vertex displacements.
