# Quantum Synchronization of Fock States

**Autori:** Fabian Hassler, David Scheer, Samah Saquaque et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30271) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30271)

## Abstract

Demonstrates phase-locking of non-classical bosonic states with negative Wigner function to external drives, showing that phase slips occur with exponentially decreasing probability.
