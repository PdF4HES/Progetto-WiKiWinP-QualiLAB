# Sharp Gaussian Isoperimetry along a Ricci Flow

**Autori:** Robert Koirala · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21193) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21193)

## Abstract

The sharp Gaussian isoperimetric inequality for conjugate heat-kernel measures along a Ricci flow is proven via a monotonicity formula. Consequences include an exact Gaussian enlargement theorem, concentration estimates, rearrangement inequalities, and applications to Poincaré inequalities and entropy stability.
