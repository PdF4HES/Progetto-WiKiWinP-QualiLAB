# Holographic X-ray Phase Contrast Imaging with Partial Coherence: Uniqueness and Reconstructions from Intensity Correlations

**Autori:** Thorsten Hohage, Milad Karimi, Björn Müller · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30146) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30146)

## Abstract

Study of holographic coherent X-ray imaging with partial coherence and time-resolved intensity measurements, demonstrating unique recovery of phase and absorption contrast under symmetry conditions using a low-rank covariance approximation approach.
