# A non-commutative discrete first Painlevé hierarchy: the Lax pair approach

**Autori:** Irina Bobrova · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29722) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29722)

## Abstract

The paper develops a non-commutative version of the discrete first Painlevé hierarchy using a non-commutative analogue of the isomonodromic problem. Both the commutative and non-commutative hierarchies are expressed through Svinin polynomials. The work demonstrates how the non-commutative Volterra lattice hierarchy reduces to this new hierarchy and provides explicit continuous limits for the first three members.
