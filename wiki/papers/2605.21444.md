# A Compression-Directional Entropic Stress Method for Shock-Regularized Compressible Flow

**Autori:** Bonan Xu, Chihyung Wen · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21444) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21444)

## Abstract

A finite-volume regularization method (CoDeS) for shock-dominated compressible flows is introduced, using tensor stress aligned with compression principal directions. The stress vanishes in smooth expansions and rigid-body rotation while providing localized shock regularization. Testing on multidimensional problems demonstrates compression-selective shock handling compatible with high-order finite-volume resolution.
