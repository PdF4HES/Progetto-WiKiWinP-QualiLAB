# Thermodynamics of homogeneous Universes: de Sitter, Bonnor-Melvin and static Einstein

**Autori:** G. E. Volovik · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21047) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21047)

## Abstract

This work constructs thermodynamics for homogeneous universes by treating gravitational fields as part of matter. Despite different matter compositions, the de Sitter, Bonnor-Melvin-Λ, and static Einstein universes exhibit identical thermodynamic properties governed by the same energy density equation.
