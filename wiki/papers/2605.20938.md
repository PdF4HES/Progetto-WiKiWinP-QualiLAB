# Origin of Persistent Boundary Motion in Confined Active Matter

**Autori:** Elsa Baby, Manoj Gopalakrishnan, Vishwas V. Vasisht · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20938) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20938)

## Abstract

Using molecular simulations and the Fokker-Planck equation, positional and orientational distributions of confined active particles are shown to be coupled. Confinement generates two preferred tangential orientational states with stochastic flipping pathways, establishing that orientational fluctuations and bistability govern dynamics in confined active systems.
