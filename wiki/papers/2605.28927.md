# Quantum encodings that preserve persistent homology

**Autori:** Arthur J. Parzygnat, Andrew Vlasic · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28927) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.28927)

## Abstract

The researchers investigate quantum encodings that preserve topological invariants from classical datasets. Rather than beginning with combinatorial objects, they propose a direct approach examining which quantum encodings acting on data are suitable for extracting topological features.
