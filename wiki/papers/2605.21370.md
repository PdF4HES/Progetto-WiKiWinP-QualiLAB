# Spectral Bernstein theorems for submanifolds in Euclidean spaces

**Autori:** Yuxin Dong, Hezi Lin, Wei Zhang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21370) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21370)

## Abstract

The essential spectrum of submanifolds in Euclidean spaces is examined under various geometric hypotheses involving extrinsic conditions such as finite total mean curvature and curvature pinching. A key result establishes that the essential spectrum of a complete non-compact submanifold equals [0, +∞) when the second fundamental form satisfies specific integrability bounds.
