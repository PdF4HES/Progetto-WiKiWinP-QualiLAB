# Orbital Stability of Smooth Traveling Solitary Waves to the Fornberg-Whitham Equation

**Autori:** Xijun Deng, Stephane Lafortune, Zhisu Liu · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19906) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19906)

## Abstract

Smooth solitary wave solutions to the Fornberg-Whitham equation, a nonlocal model for unidirectional shallow water waves, are examined. Though not completely integrable like related shallow-water equations, the FW equation supports peaked traveling waves. Using variational approaches, orbital stability of certain smooth solitary waves is demonstrated.
