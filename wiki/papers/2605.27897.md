# Super integrable hierarchies associated with color Lie algebra

**Autori:** Bo Yuan, Yanhui Bi, Yuqi Ruan et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27897) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27897)

## Abstract

The researchers construct (1+1)-dimensional and (2+1)-dimensional super integrable systems using non-isospectral problems with matrices from the color Lie algebra sp_1(6). They also establish super Hamiltonian structures through the supertrace identity.
