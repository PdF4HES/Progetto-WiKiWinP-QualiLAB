# Residual properties of finitely generated groups in the Weihrauch lattice

**Autori:** Emmanuel Rauzy · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30138) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30138)

## Abstract

Examines residual properties via the Weihrauch lattice, characterizing equational noetherianity and quasivariety membership for residually-C groups across finite, nilpotent, and other group classes.
