# A Multiscale Kinetic Framework for Image Segmentation: From Particle Systems to Continuum Models

**Autori:** Horacio Tettamanti, Giulia Guicciardi, Mattia Zanella · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28619) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.28619)

## Abstract

In this work, we present a multiscale kinetic framework for consensus-based image segmentation. By interpreting an image as a system of interacting particles, each pixel is characterised by its spatial position and an internal feature encoding color information. We introduce a coupled interaction scheme governing the evolution of particles in both position and feature spaces, from which we derive a kinetic formulation combining transport, aggregation, and diffusion effects. Based on this reduced-complexity model, we present a data-oriented approach using particle-based optimisation techniques for accurate segmentation. Numerical tests show the effectiveness of the proposed framework and its robustness under different noise conditions.
