# Classification of Single and Mixed Partial Discharges under Switching Voltage Using an AWA-CNN Framework

**Autori:** Md Rafid Kaysar Shagor et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21352) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21352)

## Abstract

Presents an Amplitude-Width-Area pattern representation for partial discharge analysis under switching-voltage excitation. CNN-based classification achieves testing accuracy above 96%, outperforming Random Forest methods for distinguishing six discharge source conditions.
