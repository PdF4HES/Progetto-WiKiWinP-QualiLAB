# Generic bundles over a localic category

**Autori:** Graham Manuell, Joshua L. Wrigley · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20407) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20407)

## Abstract

Classifying localic categories and groupoids for various bundles equipped with logical structure are constructed. When bundles are local homeomorphisms, the localic groupoids classifying geometric theories are recovered, demonstrating a stronger universal property than that of their classifying toposes. A constructive, pointfree version of the Alexandroff-Hausdorff theorem is also proven.
