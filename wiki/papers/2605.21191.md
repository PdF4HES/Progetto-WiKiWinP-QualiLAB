# Beyond Vorticity: An Angular Momentum Perspective on Fluid Flow

**Autori:** Ahmed Farooq · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21191) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21191)

## Abstract

A framework based on angular momentum density complementing classical vorticity analysis is presented. The approach provides decomposition of viscous torque, explains lift generation and stall mechanisms, reformulates hydrodynamic impulse, enables viscous added mass calculation, and simplifies geophysical fluid dynamics through explicit torque balances.
