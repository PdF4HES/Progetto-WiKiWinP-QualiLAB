# Conformation dynamics in asymmetric chain-like three-body bead-spring models

**Autori:** Yuki Sogo, Yoshiyuki Y. Yamaguchi · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29356) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29356)

## Abstract

We consider conformation dynamics of a chain-like three-body bead-spring model, in which three point masses are connected in series by two springs and conformation is defined by the bending angle between the two springs. We extend previous studies of symmetric cases into asymmetric systems, which can induce nontrivial energy exchange between modes and corresponding nontrivial conformation dynamics.
