# Effective Roles between Sperm Head and Tail on the Motility

**Autori:** Robert L. Scott, Shobitha Unnikrishnan, Ayomide Bolaji et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29050) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29050)

## Abstract

The research demonstrates that sperm movement direction deviates from wave propagation patterns when navigating viscoelastic fluids near solid substrates. Head oscillation synchronizes with movement direction while tail wave direction operates out of phase. Analysis reveals dynamic, time-varying contributions from head orientation and flagellar waves to overall motility, with the final flagellar bend remaining stationary relative to the lab frame. These findings suggest both sperm head and tail play distinct directional roles in propulsion mechanics.
