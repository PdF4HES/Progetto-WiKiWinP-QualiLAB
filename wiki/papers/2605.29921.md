# Modular invariance of characters of quasi-lisse vertex algebras

**Autori:** Tomoyuki Arakawa, Jethro van Ekeren, Hao Li · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29921) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29921)

## Abstract

Investigates conformal blocks associated with line bundles over elliptic curves. For vertex algebras meeting finiteness conditions, the authors prove the holonomicity of the sheaf of conformal blocks and generalize Zhu's theorem substantially.
