# Measurement Selection Strategies for Position Estimation in Indoor Environments

**Autori:** Neetu R. R et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19406) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19406)

## Abstract

Time-based indoor positioning techniques rely on multiple access points and measurements. Occlusion-induced non-line-of-sight propagation introduces significant delays, degrading position estimation accuracy. AP neighborhood-based cardinality selection strategies and ray-tracing-based AP neighborhood information are proposed to improve position estimation accuracy under NLoS conditions.
