# p-Adic Asymptotic Subalgebra Enumeration

**Autori:** Tomas Reunbrouck · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20422) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20422)

## Abstract

Development of p-adic asymptotics for finite-index subgroup and subalgebra enumeration. Establishes smallest real poles for local zeta functions of residually nilpotent algebras and proves conjectures regarding asymptotic behavior.
