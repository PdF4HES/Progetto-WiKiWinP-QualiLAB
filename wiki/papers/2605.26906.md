**Autori:** Serhii Bardyla, Luna Elliott · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26906) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.26906)

## Abstract

Investigates Polish semigroup topologies on endomorphism monoids, introducing property XX for automatic continuity and analyzing Zariski and pointwise topologies on various monoid structures.
