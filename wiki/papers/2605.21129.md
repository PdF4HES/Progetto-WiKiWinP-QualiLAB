# How hate spreads online and why it returns: Re-entrant phases driven by collective behavior

**Autori:** Chen Xu, Pak Ming Hui, Chenkai Xia, Neil F. Johnson · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21129) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21129)

## Abstract

Two-species coalescence-fragmentation model with SIR dynamics analyzing hate content propagation across platforms. Incorporates community linking, platform clustering, and moderator shutdowns. Analysis reveals re-entrant threshold phases governing system-wide spreading, with implications for content moderation policy effectiveness.
