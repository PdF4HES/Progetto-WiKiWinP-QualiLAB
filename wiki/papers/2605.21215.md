# Revisiting b and d through Interval Structures

**Autori:** Miguel A. Cardona, Adam Marton · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21215) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21215)

## Abstract

Relational systems from interval partitions of ω are studied, inspired by characterizations of bounding and dominating numbers. Universal variants coincide with classical invariants while existential variants systematically reverse them.
