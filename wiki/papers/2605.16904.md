# Positive-rate PCA and IPS with stationary Bernoulli measures are rapidly forgetful

**Autori:** Irène Marcovici, Siamak Taati · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16904) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.16904)

## Abstract

Every probabilistic cellular automaton with strictly positive transition probabilities admitting a stationary Bernoulli measure exhibits exponential ergodicity, with finite region mixing times logarithmic in region diameter. Similar results apply to positive-rate interacting particle systems. Ergodic behavior results from competition between randomness accumulation and local information diffusion.
