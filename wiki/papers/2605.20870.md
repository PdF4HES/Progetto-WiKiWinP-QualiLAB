# Development of a xenon triple point apparatus suitable for calibrating long-stem SPRTs

**Autori:** Yikun Zhao et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20870) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20870)

## Abstract

The xenon triple point is a promising candidate for replacing the mercury triple point in the international temperature scale ITS-90. The study develops an immersion-type xenon triple point apparatus suitable for calibration of both long-stem and capsule standard platinum resistance thermometers. Melting plateaus extend 8–12 hours with temperature flatness of 0.37–1.0 mK. Two measurements yield 161.405 71(55) K at melted fraction F=1.0.
