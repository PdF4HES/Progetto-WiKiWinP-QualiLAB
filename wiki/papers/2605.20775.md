# Pseudo-Euclidean Novikov Superalgebras: Structure and Properties

**Autori:** Said Benayadi, Sofiane Bouarroudj, Hamza El Ouali · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20775) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20775)

## Abstract

Study of pseudo-Euclidean Novikov superalgebras with non-degenerate symmetric bilinear forms where left multiplication operators are antisymmetric. Introduces Milnor superalgebras and double extension procedures, establishing classification for dimensions up to four.
