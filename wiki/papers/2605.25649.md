# What if we decompose a simple tone? The Chinese remainder theorem and structured Levi graphs in music

**Autori:** Paweł Nurowski · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.25649) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.25649)

## Abstract

Introduces a novel combinatorial framework that advances the classification of cyclic cubic bipartite graphs by treating vertices as algebraic subsets, applying this to tone networks via the Chinese Remainder Theorem in 12-TET and 10-TET systems.
