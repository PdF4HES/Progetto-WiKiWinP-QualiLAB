# WikiVQABench: A Knowledge-Grounded Visual Question Answering Benchmark from Wikipedia and Wikidata

**Autori:** Basel Shbita, Pengyuan Li, Anna Lisa Gentile · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21479) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21479)

## Abstract

WikiVQABench is a human-curated knowledge-grounded VQA benchmark constructed by combining Wikipedia images, article captions, and structured knowledge from Wikidata. Uses LLMs to generate multiple-choice instances reviewed by human annotators. Evaluation of fifteen VLMs (256M-90B parameters) reveals a wide performance range (24.7%-75.6% accuracy).
