# Energetic characterisation of transient clustering dynamics in aggregation-diffusion systems

**Autori:** Nathalie Wehlitz, Richard Scherzer, Carsten Hartmann et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30243) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30243)

## Abstract

Investigates transient clustering in nonlocal aggregation-diffusion systems through Wasserstein gradient-flow analysis. Identifies alternating aggregation- and diffusion-dominated regimes in particle systems converging to equilibria.
