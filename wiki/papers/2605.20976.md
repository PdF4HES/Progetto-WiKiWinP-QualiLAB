# A compensation theorem for the Sylow-integral invariant and counterexamples to an A5-characterization conjecture

**Autori:** Yutong Zhang, Yaoran Yang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20976) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20976)

## Abstract

The authors disprove a conjecture about the Sylow-integral invariant by constructing a non-isomorphic nonsolvable group with the same invariant value as A5, using an explicit compensation formula for direct products.
