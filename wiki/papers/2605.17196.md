# Maxwell's Demon

**Autori:** R. E. Kastner · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.17196) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.17196)

## Abstract

An overview of key historical developments in formulating the Second Law of Thermodynamics, focusing on Maxwell's Demon. The paper recalls Maxwell's challenge and discusses alternative formulations by Szilard and Brillouin, along with Bennett's proposal to defeat the Demon through memory erasure requirements. The work proposes that the Second Law gains a firm foundation through the Heisenberg Uncertainty Principle, which decisively defeats the Demon and justifies Landauer's Principle.
