# Generalisable presentations and compactness properties of locally compact right-angled Artin groups

**Autori:** Ilaria Castellano, Bianca Marchionna, Brita Nucinkis et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28963) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.28963)

## Abstract

The study introduces topological right-angled Artin groups and examines presentations generalizable over continuous open group monomorphisms. The work investigates geometric aspects, universal Salvetti-type complexes, and compactness properties of locally compact groups, yielding novel examples with prescribed finiteness properties.
