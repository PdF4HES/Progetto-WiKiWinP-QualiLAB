# A solution to the Yau-Tian-Donaldson Conjecture through Special Fujita Approximations

**Autori:** Antonio Trusiani · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30063) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30063)

## Abstract

Demonstrates special Fujita approximations for big line bundles and resolves the Yau-Tian-Donaldson Conjecture by establishing equivalence between cscK metrics and K-stability for polarized varieties.
