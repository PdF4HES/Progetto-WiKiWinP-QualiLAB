# Monodromy free Schrödinger operators and affine sl_2 master functions

**Autori:** Andrei Grigorev, Evgeny Mukhin · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30164) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30164)

## Abstract

Studies Fuchsian differential operators where the operator L+λP(x) is monodromy free for all complex λ, proving such operators derive from critical points of affine master functions and obtaining classifications for polynomial cases.
