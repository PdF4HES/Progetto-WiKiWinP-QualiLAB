**Autori:** Adil Kabylda, Malte Esders, Matteo Gori et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28960) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.28960)

## Abstract

Using quantum field theory and machine learning force fields, researchers investigate interatomic forces in large molecular systems, finding that interaction scatter remains robust and exhibits substantial anisotropy.
