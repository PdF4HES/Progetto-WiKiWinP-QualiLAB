# Complex network topological and spectral determinants of extreme events

**Autori:** Christian Hechler, Timo Bröhl, Ulrike Feudel et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29767) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29767)

## Abstract

We study the impact of coupling topology on the ability of networked dynamical systems to generate extreme events. By determining the coupling strength necessary to generate an extreme event in collective dynamics, we observe a power-law-like relationship between this coupling threshold and both topological (edge density) and spectral (algebraic connectivity) properties of various coupling topologies. This relationship appears largely independent of both the investigated system and the underlying mechanism to generate extreme events, suggesting the relationship is primarily mediated by coupling topology aspects.
