# Task-specific programming of chaos in neural circuits

**Autori:** Jungyoon Kim et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19465) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19465)

## Abstract

Chaotic dynamics serve as a versatile resource for neuromorphic and probabilistic computing, enabling high-dimensional nonlinear processing. Using a continuous-time neural-circuit model, programmable chaotic dynamics for task-specific reservoir computing are demonstrated. Tuning network topology drives an ordered-to-chaotic transition. Small-world connectivity enables low-latency on-off switching of chaos via edge rewiring.
