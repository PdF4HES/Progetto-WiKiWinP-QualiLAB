# Cloud-Native Operation of Roadside Infrastructure Enabling Demand-Driven Collective Perception via V2X

**Autori:** Lukas Zanger et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21145) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21145)

## Abstract

Presents a cloud-native architecture for roadside infrastructure based on Kubernetes with demand-driven orchestration. Demonstrates that collective perception applications deploy in time for connected vehicles while reducing energy consumption and channel congestion when inactive.
