# Formation of mechanical rogue waves

**Autori:** Yasuhiro Miyazawa et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18518) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.18518)

## Abstract

Rogue waves, characterized by extreme abrupt localization in space and time, have been studied across multiple fields but remain experimentally elusive in mechanical systems. This work reports experimental formation of mechanical rogue waves in a one-dimensional metamaterial lattice with tailored nonlinearity and minimal dissipation. The emergence of these extreme events requires synergy between high nonlinearity and broad spatial energy reservoirs.
