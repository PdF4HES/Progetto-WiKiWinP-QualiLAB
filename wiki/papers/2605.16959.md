# Over-approximation of weakly-hard constraints for control systems verification

**Autori:** Rieke de Maeyer, Holger Hermanns, Martina Maggio · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16959) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.16959)

## Abstract

A compressed language acceptor for weakly-hard real-time constraints that enables verification of control systems with deadline misses. The method provides sound safety guarantees while reducing the state machine size.
