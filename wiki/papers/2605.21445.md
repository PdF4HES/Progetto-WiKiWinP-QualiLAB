# Error analysis of a finite element scheme for parametric mean curvature flow based on the DeTurck trick

**Autori:** Klaus Deckelnick, Vanessa Styles · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21445) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21445)

## Abstract

The paper analyzes a numerical scheme for approximating parametric mean curvature flow using DeTurck reparametrization. For semidiscrete finite element spatial discretization of order k ≥ 2, optimal H¹-error estimates for the position vector are proven. Numerical experiments confirm the error bound and demonstrate good mesh point distribution properties.
