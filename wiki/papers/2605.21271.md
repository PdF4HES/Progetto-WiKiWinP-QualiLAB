# Dark solitons in the fractional NLS equation

**Autori:** Almudena P. Márquez, Jesús Cuevas-Maraver, Panayotis G. Kevrekidis · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21271) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21271)

## Abstract

This work examines dark fractional solitary waves in generalized fractional nonlinear Schrödinger equation settings. The study demonstrates existence and stability of individual dark solitary waves and two-soliton solutions, finding different equilibrium branches. All two-soliton branches exhibit instability — odd branches show oscillatory instabilities while even branches are exponentially unstable.
