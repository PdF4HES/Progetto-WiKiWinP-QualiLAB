# On the Complexity of Hop Domination and 2-Step Domination in Graph Classes

**Autori:** Sandip Das, Sweta Das, Sk Samim Islam · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20970) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20970)

## Abstract

This work investigates computational complexity of hop domination and 2-step domination problems, showing both are NP-complete even when restricted to d-regular graphs, claw-free graphs, and unit disk graphs.
