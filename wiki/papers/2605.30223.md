# Hodge numbers of moduli of principal bundles on a curve

**Autori:** Chiu-Chu Melissa Liu, Florent Schaffhauser · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30223) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30223)

## Abstract

The authors prove an inversion theorem for recursive formulas and derive a closed formula for the Hodge-Poincaré series of moduli stacks of semistable principal G-bundles.
