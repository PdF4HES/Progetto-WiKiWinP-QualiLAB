# Dark-Soliton Branch Blocking in Transonic Bose-Einstein Condensate Flows

**Autori:** Edilberto O. Silva · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27863) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27863)

## Abstract

Acoustic horizons in Bose-Einstein condensates are usually characterized through long-wavelength Bogoliubov phonons. This work studies a nonlinear counterpart: whether a one-dimensional dark-soliton branch can sustain upstream laboratory motion in a stationary transonic flow. The research examines how dark solitons interact with sonic points in quantum fluid dynamics, presenting simulations of full order parameter evolution and demonstrating that soliton propagation is constrained by local sound speed limits.
