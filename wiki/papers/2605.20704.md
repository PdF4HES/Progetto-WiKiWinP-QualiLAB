# Heartbeat-Bound Hierarchical Credentials: Cryptographic Revocation for AI Agent Swarms

**Autori:** Saurabh Deochake · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20704) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20704)

## Abstract

A cryptographic protocol binding credential validity to periodic parent liveness proofs, addressing credential revocation for autonomous AI agent swarms. The approach achieves a 90x reduction in the zombie window compared to OAuth 2.0.
