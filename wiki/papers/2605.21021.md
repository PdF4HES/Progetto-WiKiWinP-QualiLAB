# Microscopic Nonaffine Deformation Theory of LAOS in Polymers

**Autori:** Dario Nichetti, Alessio Zaccone · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21021) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21021)

## Abstract

A molecularly motivated framework connecting large-amplitude oscillatory shear nonlinearities in entangled polymers to frequency-dependent nonaffine relaxation. The first harmonic measures residual phase-locked elastic response while higher harmonics encode strain-dependent relaxation signatures. Results establish connections between tube-based polymer dynamics, harmonic analysis, elastoplastic rheology, and microscopic nonaffine lattice dynamics.
