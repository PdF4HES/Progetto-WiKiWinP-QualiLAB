# Verification of Configurable SRA Systems

**Autori:** Alessandro Cimatti et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21385) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21385)

## Abstract

Addresses verification of scheduler-restricted asynchronous systems using contract-based deductive approaches with compositional proof rules and automatic method summarizations, leveraging Dafny verifier on industrial case studies.
