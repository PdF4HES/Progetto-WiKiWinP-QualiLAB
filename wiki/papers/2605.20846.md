# Topological Field Theories and the Algebraic Structures of the Two-Sphere

**Autori:** Chris Li · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20846) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20846)

## Abstract

Presentations for bordisms of S² in 3-dimensional oriented bordism category are provided, defining P-monoids and L-monoids equipped with endomorphisms labeled by closed oriented irreducible prime 3-manifolds. An ∞-operad encoding these prime structures is proposed, containing the ∞-little 3-cube operad as a sub-operad.
