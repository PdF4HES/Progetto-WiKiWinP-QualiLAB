# Similarity Solutions for the Flux limited Keller-Segel System with Time Varying Chemical Decay Rate

**Autori:** Ahmed Abbas Jaber Al Furaiji et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21394) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21394)

## Abstract

A one-dimensional flux limited Keller-Segel system with time-varying chemical decay rate is investigated through Lie symmetry analysis. The study performs complete group classification and identifies three distinguished cases extending symmetry algebra. Similarity reductions and explicit solutions are derived for flux limited chemotaxis models.
