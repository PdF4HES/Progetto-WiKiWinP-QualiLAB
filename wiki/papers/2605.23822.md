# Coarse Structures on Homogeneous Spaces

**Autori:** Carlos Pérez Estrada, Christian Rosendal · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.23822) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.23822)

## Abstract

Addresses whether quotient coarse structures equal coarse structures of quotient groups, providing counterexamples and sufficient conditions using bounded set liftings and transversals.
