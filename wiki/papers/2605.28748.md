# Filtering out Erratic Observables: Wormholes from Gauging Nonlocal Symmetries

**Autori:** Qi-Feng Wu · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28748) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.28748)

## Abstract

Interprets wormhole contributions to gravitational path integrals by examining boundary gravitons in (2+1)-dimensional gravity, showing that gauging the nonlocal symmetries is equivalent to filtering out the erratic observables.
