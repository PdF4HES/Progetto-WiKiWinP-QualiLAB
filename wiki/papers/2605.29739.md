# Inverse generalised spin models of answers to questionnaires

**Autori:** Arianna Armanetti, Luca Cecchetti, Paolo Sarti et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29739) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29739)

## Abstract

Infers and analyzes generalized spin models (Ising, Blume-Capel, BEG) of ordinal questionnaire data, demonstrating that the BEG model systematically outperforms other models at capturing response distributions.
