# The Wasserstein cost of Importance Sampling

**Autori:** Simon Coste, Michael Goldman · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30055) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30055)

## Abstract

Analysis demonstrating that the Wasserstein cost W_p^p(ĝ_n, g) has order n^{-p/d} in expectation for importance sampling in high dimensions, with optimal sampling distribution results.
