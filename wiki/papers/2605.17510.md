# Scale-Dependent Collective Adaptation in Self-Amending LLM Societies: A Cross-Family Study of Emergent Governance

**Autori:** Kazuya Horibe et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.17510) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.17510)

## Abstract

Investigation of group decision-making in artificial societies using the self-amending game Nomic across LLM scales. Findings show non-monotonic collective adaptation: smaller models remain rule-inert, larger models converge on restrictive patterns, while optimal mid-scale regimes support sustained rule adoption and balanced consensus.
