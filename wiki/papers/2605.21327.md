# Universal fusion category symmetries on tensor products of infinite-dimensional Hilbert spaces

**Autori:** Ian Bunner, Corey Jones · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21327) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21327)

## Abstract

Establishes that anyon chains with infinite-dimensional ancilla spaces realize unitary fusion categories as lattice symmetries, proving unique stable equivalence classes for fixed fusion categories.
