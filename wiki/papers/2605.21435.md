# Gaussian Sheaf Neural Networks

**Autori:** André Ribeiro et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21435) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21435)

## Abstract

Graph Neural Networks have become standard for learning on relational data. While traditional GNNs' message passing suits vector-valued node features, cases exist where features are better represented by probability distributions. When node features are Gaussians with mean and covariance matrix, naive concatenation discards governing geometric and algebraic structure. We propose Gaussian Sheaf Neural Networks, a framework incorporating these inductive biases into graph-based learning. Building on cellular sheaf theory, we derive a new Laplacian operator generalizing the sheaf Laplacian and preserving its key properties.
