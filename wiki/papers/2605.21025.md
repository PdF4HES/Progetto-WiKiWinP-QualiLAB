# Termination of the Lattice-Automorphism Tower for Direct Products of Symmetric Groups

**Autori:** Sonukumar, Vinay Madhusudanan · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21025) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21025)

## Abstract

The paper establishes a product formula for lattice automorphisms of direct products of symmetric groups and proves a termination theorem showing that the LatAut tower reaches the trivial group at stage 3, with this bound being sharp.
