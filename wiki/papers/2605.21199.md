# Ergodicity of (co)expanding on average random dynamical systems

**Autori:** Jonathan DeWitt, Dmitry Dolgopyat, Zhiyuan Zhang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21199) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21199)

## Abstract

Ergodicity is proven for random dynamics satisfying expansion and irreducibility conditions. For random dynamics of two rotations generating a dense subgroup on spheres, stable ergodicity is demonstrated, with applications to spectral gap and statistical limit theorems.
