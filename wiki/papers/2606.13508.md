# Silting subcategories and (co)torsion pairs associated to extended hearts

**Autori:** Liangwei Huang, Haicheng Zhang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13508) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.13508)

## Abstract

We establish the poset isomorphisms between $(d+1)$-term silting subcategories, functorially finite $s$-torsion pairs in the $d$-extended heart, and hereditary complete cotorsion pairs in a suitable subcategory. As an application, we also give dg algebra versions of these bijections, which establish the poset isomorphisms between $τ$-tilting pairs, $(d+1)$-term silting complexes, and functorially finite $s$-torsion pairs.
