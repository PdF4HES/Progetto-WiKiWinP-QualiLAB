# Memorisation, convergence and generalisation in generative models

**Autori:** Antoine Maillard, Sebastian Goldt · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21402) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21402)

## Abstract

Generative neural networks learn from finite training examples, raising questions about memorisation versus generalisation. This work provides analytical characterisation of the transition from memorisation to generalisation in linear generative models, finding that convergence emerges when sample numbers are linear in input dimension. The analysis reveals that generalisation decomposes into two distinct objectives: matching data distribution and recovering principal latent factors.
