**Autori:** Søren Brinck Knudstorp · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29880) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29880)

## Abstract

We prove undecidability for every positive relevant logic extending specific axioms, settling the decision problem for semilattice relevant logic S negatively.
