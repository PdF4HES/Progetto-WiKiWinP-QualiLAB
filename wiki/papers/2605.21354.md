# Micro-explosion of emulsion droplets with nanoparticles at high temperature

**Autori:** Houpeng Zhang et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21354) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21354)

## Abstract

An experimental investigation of micro-explosion in emulsion droplets containing nanoparticles. Results show nanoparticles significantly enhance micro-explosion strength and probability through agglomeration, radiation absorption, and vapor diffusion inhibition. Effects of nanoparticle mass fraction and water content on micro-explosion are examined.
