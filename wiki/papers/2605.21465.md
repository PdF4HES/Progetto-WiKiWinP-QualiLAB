# Leveraging LLMs for Grammar Adaptation: A Study on Metamodel-Grammar Co-Evolution

**Autori:** Weixing Zhang et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21465) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21465)

## Abstract

Proposes LLM-based approach for automating grammar adaptation after metamodel evolution in model-driven engineering. Achieves superior consistency compared to rule-based methods on standard benchmarks, though faces limitations with large-scale grammars.
