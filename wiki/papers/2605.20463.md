# Effective strains enable rapid wound closure in jellyfish after injury

**Autori:** Anne Materne, Zhiqi Shen, Chiara Sinigaglia, Carl D. Modes · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20463) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20463)

## Abstract

An in silico spring lattice model demonstrates that radially contractile pre-strains within jellyfish tissue initiate rapid wound closure across wide conditions, even for large injuries. Analytical expressions quantify achievable closure as a function of residual material geometry.
