# That Damned Equation. Rigour, Credit Attribution, and the Wheeler-DeWitt Equation 1962-1967

**Autori:** Alexander S. Blum, Dean Rickles, Karim Thébault · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29696) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29696)

## Abstract

Historical case study examining how rigor in theoretical physics aims at rendering formally well-defined models enabling concrete calculations rather than pursuing abstract mathematical truth. Traces the credit attribution dispute around the Wheeler-DeWitt equation.
