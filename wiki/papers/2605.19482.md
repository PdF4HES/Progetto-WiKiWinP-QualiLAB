# Methodological Synergies between Technosignature and UHE Neutrino Searches

**Autori:** Paramita Dasgupta · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19482) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19482)

## Abstract

Radio technosignature searches and UHE neutrino experiments share a common data analysis challenge: identifying rare signals within datasets dominated by thermal noise and RFI. Advanced methodologies from UHE neutrino experiments (ARA, RNO-G, ANITA, PUEO) are adapted for technosignature pipelines, proposing joint feature spaces and background-only anomaly ranking.
