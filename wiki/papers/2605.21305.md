# Tverberg cores and Kalai's cascade conjecture

**Autori:** Pablo Soberón · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21305) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21305)

## Abstract

Studies topological analogues of Kalai's cascade conjecture. For continuous maps from n-simplices to Euclidean space, establishes conditions on dimension and parameters ensuring r-Tverberg points persist after vertex deletion, confirming the cascade conjecture for finite point sets.
