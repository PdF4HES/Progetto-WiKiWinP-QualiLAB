# Well-posedness and asymptotic limits for a degenerate Keller-Segel system with volume filling

**Autori:** Noah Geltner, Ansgar Jüngel, Mingyue Zhang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21296) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21296)

## Abstract

Parabolic-parabolic Keller-Segel systems with degenerate diffusion and volume filling are studied. The work establishes global weak solution existence, weak-strong uniqueness, exponential convergence to homogeneous steady states, and pattern formation analysis. Asymptotic limits including parabolic-elliptic and vanishing diffusion regimes are examined.
