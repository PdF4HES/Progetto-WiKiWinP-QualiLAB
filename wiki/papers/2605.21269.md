# Transforming Privacy Artifacts into Accessible Reports for Non-Technical Stakeholders

**Autori:** Zoe Pfister et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21269) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21269)

## Abstract

The transition toward Industry 5.0 emphasizes human-centric collaboration between humans and machines. This paper proposes a framework leveraging Large Language Models to transform technical privacy artifacts into accessible reports for non-technical stakeholders, supporting informed decision-making in human-centric industrial systems.
