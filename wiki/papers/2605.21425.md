# Achieving Material Robustness via Symmetric Stress Finite Element Discretizations

**Autori:** Pablo Brubeck, Charles Parker, Umberto Zerbinati · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21425) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21425)

## Abstract

Study of enforcing stress tensor symmetry in finite element discretizations. Demonstrates that strong enforcement yields accurate stress approximations across anisotropic constitutive laws, termed material robustness, while weak enforcement can fail arbitrarily.
