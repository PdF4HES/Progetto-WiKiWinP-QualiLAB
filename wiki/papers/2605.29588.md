# Brain-IT-VQA: From Brain Signals to Answers

**Autori:** Roman Beliy, Matias Cosarinsky, Oliver Heinimann et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29588) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29588)

## Abstract

Brain-IT-VQA is a framework for visual question answering from fMRI that decodes language tokens from brain activity and integrates them with a language model. The authors introduce NSD-VQA, a new dataset providing approximately 20 question-answer pairs per image across 20 controlled question categories, enabling more reliable evaluation of visual understanding decoding from brain responses.
