# Hidden Ising models from the generalized Yang-Baxter equation

**Autori:** Akash Sinha, Somnath Maity, Pramod Padmanabhan et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30007) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30007)

## Abstract

The researchers introduce a one-dimensional spin-1/2 system with multi-site local interactions. The Hamiltonian density algebra mirrors that of the transverse field Ising model, yielding a free-fermionic spectrum with extensive degeneracy. Local conserved quantities function as a classical background field. At gapless spectral points, the system emerges from the quantum inverse scattering method using a multi-site generalization of the Yang-Baxter equation. The R-matrix employs extraspecial 2-group generators to extract conserved charges and establish a framework for generating similar spin systems. The work also addresses connections to Fendley's free-fermion models.
