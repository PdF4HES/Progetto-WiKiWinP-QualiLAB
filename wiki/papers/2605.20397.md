# An ℓ² Obstruction for Elementary Embeddings of Hyperbolic Groups

**Autori:** Connor MacMahon · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20397) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20397)

## Abstract

Shows first ℓ² Betti numbers are non-decreasing under embeddings from first-order logic. Proves strict inequality for elementary embeddings of non-abelian proper subgroups in torsion-free hyperbolic groups.
