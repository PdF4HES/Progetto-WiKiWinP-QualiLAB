# Dynamical systems on ultra small-world networks

**Autori:** Nirbhay Patil, Ada Altieri, Fabian Aguirre-Lopez · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21364) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21364)

## Abstract

A framework extends dynamical mean-field theory to highly heterogeneous networks accounting for degree correlations from structural cut-offs. Applied to the disordered Lotka-Volterra model, the approach analyzes survival rates and stability on ultra small-world networks. Results show improved agreement across power-law network exponent ranges and empirical networks compared to typical reported observables.
