# The Baire property in uniform spaces: a survey

**Autori:** Francisco Javier García Pacheco et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18225) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.18225)

## Abstract

The Baire category theorem states that every complete pseudometric space is a Baire space. While results in metric spaces often have analogues in uniform spaces, this is not one of them. Conditions such as countable compacity, pseudocompacity and pseudocompleteness are explored to determine in which circumstances a general complete uniform space satisfies the Baire property.
