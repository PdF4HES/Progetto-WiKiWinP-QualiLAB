**Autori:** Shengkai Mao, Peihang Wu · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30086) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30086)

## Abstract

Study constructs canonical extensions of p-adic shtukas on integral models of toroidal compactifications of abelian-type Shimura varieties with quasi-parahoric levels.
