# Evidence of Quantum Machine Learning Advantage with Tens of Noisy Qubits

**Autori:** Onur Danaci et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21346) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21346)

## Abstract

Learning problems involving quantum data offer natural opportunities for demonstrating quantum machine learning advantages. This work presents simulations analyzing performance on existing hardware for learning tasks exhibiting asymptotic advantage, with noisy quantum data. Comparing coherent quantum processing against fixed-measurement schemes demonstrates clear performance separation at 30–40 noisy qubits. Demonstrating strong learning advantages appears accessible on near-term devices.
