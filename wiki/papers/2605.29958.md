# Lattice Brownian bees with cooperative reproduction: steady states, collapse, and spreading

**Autori:** Ohad Vilk, Baruch Meerson · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29958) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29958)

## Abstract

The researchers extended a previously established model to include cooperative reproduction among N symmetric random walkers. Their hydrodynamic analysis reveals that steady state densities vary with the reproduction parameter k. For k≤2, these states are linearly stable; for k≥4, they become unstable. The critical case k=3 exhibits a continuous family of steady states. Above criticality, populations experience rapid collapse toward the origin, while below it they spread through diffusion. Unstable states separate collapse from spreading regimes. The findings are validated through both hydrodynamic problem solutions and Monte Carlo simulations.
