# AIMBio-Mat: An AI-Native FAIR Platform for Closed-Loop Materials Discovery and Biomedical Translation

**Autori:** D.-M. Mei et al. (50+ autori) · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21083) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21083)

## Abstract

An AI-native platform blueprint integrating materials provenance, biomedical context, and uncertainty-aware machine learning for discovery. The framework formulates biomedical-materials discovery as constrained multi-objective optimization, includes governance-aware decision layers, and provides a prototype specification with nanomaterials pilot implementation.
