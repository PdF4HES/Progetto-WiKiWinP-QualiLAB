# Nonlinear Dynamics of Rapidly Driven Systems

**Autori:** Afshin Besharat, Alexander A. Penin · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28996) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.28996)

## Abstract

We consider systems characterized by a rapidly oscillating force. A general method is presented for constructing the effective action governing large-scale nonlinear dynamics order by order in inverse powers of oscillation frequency. The explicit expression for the effective Lagrangian is derived up to next-to-next-to-leading approximation. The method enables fully nonperturbative stability analysis in cases of strong driving and nonlinearity, with applications including dynamical magnetic trapping of electric charges.
