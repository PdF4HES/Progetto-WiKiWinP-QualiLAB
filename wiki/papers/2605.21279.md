# Composition operators for holomorphic Lipschitz functions

**Autori:** Verónica Dimant et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21279) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21279)

## Abstract

We study composition operators on spaces of holomorphic Lipschitz functions defined on the open unit ball of a complex Banach space. Through holomorphic Lipschitz-free spaces, composition operators are realized as adjoints of linear operators. For spaces with the bounded approximation property, composition operators between spaces vanishing at the origin are characterized, and compactness properties are investigated.
