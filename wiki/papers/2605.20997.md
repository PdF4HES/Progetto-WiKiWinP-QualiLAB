# Hybrid Machine Learning Model for Forest Height Estimation from TanDEM-X and Landsat Data

**Autori:** Islam Mansour et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20997) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20997)

## Abstract

A ML model for estimating forest height from TanDEM-X interferometric coherence is extended with optical Landsat data for complementary information on forest type or structure. Applied and validated on TanDEM-X acquisitions over the Gabonese Lopé national park and assessed against airborne LiDAR measurements. Results show a 13.5% reduction in RMSE and a 16.6% reduction in MAE compared to the original hybrid model.
