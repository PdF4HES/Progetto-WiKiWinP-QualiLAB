# CandorMD: An AI-Assisted Audio Simulation and Feedback System for Training Clinicians for Medical Error Disclosure

**Autori:** Inna Wanyin Lin et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20701) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20701)

## Abstract

CandorMD is an AI-assisted simulation system designed to train physicians in medical error disclosure conversations. It provides real-time practice, actionable feedback, and tailored learning environments based on stakeholder input from clinicians and patient advocates.
