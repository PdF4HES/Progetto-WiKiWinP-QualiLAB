# A random walk approach to high-dimensional critical phenomena

**Autori:** Hugo Duminil-Copin et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21438) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21438)

## Abstract

A unified probabilistic proof of mean-field near-critical behavior for lattice statistical mechanical models above upper critical dimensions, applicable to self-avoiding walks, percolation, spin systems, and lattice trees.
