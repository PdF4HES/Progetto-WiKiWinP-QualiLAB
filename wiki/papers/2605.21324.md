# Stimulus symmetries can confound representational similarity analyses

**Autori:** Farhad Pashakhanloo, Jacob A. Zavatone-Veth · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21324) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21324)

## Abstract

Stimulus symmetries in network inputs can confound representational similarity matrix analyses. Functionally equivalent representations produce different RSMs reflecting qualitatively different geometries, with implications for comparing nonlinear neural codes.
