# Compensator-Based Inference for Signal Detection Under Unknown Background

**Autori:** Aritra Banerjee, Sara Algeri · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20508) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20508)

## Abstract

This work addresses signal detection amid unknown background, common in scientific discovery. Rather than estimating background distribution, the approach demonstrates that estimating a single compensator parameter suffices to account for incomplete background knowledge. This simplifies problem complexity while enabling proper uncertainty propagation.
