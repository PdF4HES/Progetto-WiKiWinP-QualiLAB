# Connection of hypocoercivity and hypocontractivity via the θ-methods

**Autori:** Anton Arnold, Stefan Egger · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30197) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30197)

## Abstract

Analyzes how hypocoercivity properties of linear evolution equations transfer to discrete approximations via θ-methods, showing implicit discretizations with θ∈(½,1] are contractive rather than hypocontractive.
