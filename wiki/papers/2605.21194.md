# Listing the hyperarithmetical functions

**Autori:** Joseph S. Miller, Gian Marco Osso, Isabella Scott · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21194) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21194)

## Abstract

For countable Turing ideals, conditions are established for when elements compute lists. Results characterize reals computing lists of HYP based on domination properties and definability of Kleene's O.
