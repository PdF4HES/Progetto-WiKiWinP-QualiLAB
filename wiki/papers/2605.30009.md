**Autori:** Carlos Garzón, Oscar Riaño · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30009) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30009)

## Abstract

Analysis of how local and nonlocal dispersions with various nonlinearities influence solution smoothing effects, demonstrating that higher-order dispersive terms determine fractional regularity gains.
