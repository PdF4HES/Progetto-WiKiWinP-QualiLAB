# Combining non-parametric quantum states and MERA tensor networks for ground-state optimization

**Autori:** Julian Schuhmacher et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21447) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21447)

## Abstract

This work proposes combining a non-parametric quantum state from quantum annealing with a classical isometric tensor network. The classical component undergoes variational optimization while the quantum component serves as a fixed boundary tensor resource via classical shadows. Numerical simulations on the transverse-field Ising model demonstrate robustness under noise and improved ground state approximation accuracy compared to original quantum simulation.
