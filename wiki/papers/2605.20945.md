# Self-simulability of right-angled Artin groups

**Autori:** Kanéda Blot, Ville Salo · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20945) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20945)

## Abstract

The authors characterize when right-angled Artin groups are self-simulable, establishing that a RAAG is self-simulable if and only if its defining graph contains no disconnecting clique.
