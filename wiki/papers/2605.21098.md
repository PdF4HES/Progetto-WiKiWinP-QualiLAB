# A strange continued fraction associated with the Romik map

**Autori:** Yufei Chen, Karma Dajani, Yanyan Hu, Cor Kraaikamp · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21098) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21098)

## Abstract

The Romik system, a modified Euclidean algorithm for Primitive Pythagorean Triples, is further studied. Properties of rational and quadratic irrational expansions are determined, and a planar natural extension with σ-finite invariant measure is obtained.
