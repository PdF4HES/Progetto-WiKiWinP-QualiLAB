# ProCrit: Self-Elicited Multi-Perspective Reasoning with Critic-Guided Revision for Multimodal Sarcasm Detection

**Autori:** Yingjia Xu et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20867) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20867)

## Abstract

ProCrit proposes a two-agent framework for multimodal sarcasm detection using self-elicited perspectives and critic-guided revision. The system synthesizes process-level reasoning annotations and employs draft-critique-revise paradigms with dual-stage reinforcement learning.
