# Activation Functions, Statistics and Learning of Higher-Order Interactions in Restricted Boltzmann Machines

**Autori:** Giovanni di Sarra, Yasser Roudi · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19178) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19178)

## Abstract

This work exploits the duality between RBMs and models of interacting binary variables to study statistics of interactions induced by RBM ensembles with different activation functions. Analysis characterizes representable model spaces for Linear, Step, ReLU, and Exponential functions, showing nonlinearities facilitate representation of complex data structures.
