# Hecke Triangle Groups and Special Hyperbolic Elements

**Autori:** Karl Winsor · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30064) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30064)

## Abstract

Studies Hecke triangle group actions, proving infinitely many distinct orbits of fixed points exist for q=18. Constructs special affine pseudo-Anosov homeomorphisms on regular polygon unfoldings.
