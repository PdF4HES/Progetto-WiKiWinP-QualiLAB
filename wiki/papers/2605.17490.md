## Abstract

Unified mesoscopic framework explaining pink noise emergence through repeated synchronization, amplitude modulation, and demodulation. Stochastic Kuramoto model produces 1/f spectra across broad parameter domains. Mechanism applies to economic data, music, earthquakes, and astrophysical systems as diagnostic of modulated collective coherence.
