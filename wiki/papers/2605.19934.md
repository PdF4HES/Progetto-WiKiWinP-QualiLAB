# Large-space and large-time asymptotics for the mKdV soliton gas with any odd genus

**Autori:** Dedi Yan, Xianguo Geng, Kedong Wang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19934) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19934)

## Abstract

Large-space and large-time asymptotic behavior of genus 2n-1 soliton gas for the mKdV equation is studied. As x approaches positive infinity, large-space asymptotics are expressed via Riemann-theta functions of genus 2n-1. Large-time analysis employing nonlinear steepest descent and g-function approaches partitions the half-plane into 2n+1 regions, each characterized by Riemann-theta function asymptotics with uniform error estimation.
