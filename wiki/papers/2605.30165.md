# Tunneling phase diagram: A machine-learning framework for multidimensional kinetic isotope effects

**Autori:** Xinrui Yang, Zhigang Wang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30165) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30165)

## Abstract

The kinetic isotope effect (KIE) is the conventional probe for quantum tunneling, yet its composite nature conflates tunneling with zero-point energy and classical kinetics. This machine-learning framework decouples quantum tunneling strength by analyzing the connection between KIE and the tunneling factor κ, revealing anomalous high KIE-low κ behavior spanning 300-600 K with exceptional accuracy (R² > 0.98).
