# The Coefficients of the C_p-Equivariant Geometric Complex Cobordism

**Autori:** Sebastian Gómez Rendón · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21323) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21323)

## Abstract

Complete calculation of the cobordism ring of stably almost complex C_p-manifolds in terms of generators and relations, with comparison to geometrically-defined generators obtained by Kosniowski.
