# Estimates of ground state energies for the quantum SK and 2D-EA model, using deGennes-Suzuki-Kubo mean-field annealing dynamics

**Autori:** Soumyaditya Das, Soumyajyoti Biswas, Bikas K. Chakrabarti · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29875) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29875)

## Abstract

Large-scale simulation of quantum annealing in Sherrington-Kirkpatrick spin glass up to N=40000 using deGennes-Suzuki-Kubo dynamics. Extends method to Edwards-Anderson spin glass with O(N³) algorithmic cost.
