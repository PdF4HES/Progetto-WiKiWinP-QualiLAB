# On the Regularity and Generalization of One-Step Wasserstein-guided Generative Models for PDE-Induced Measures

**Autori:** Likun Lin et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21388) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21388)

## Abstract

Theoretical framework for transport map regularity and generalization of Wasserstein-guided generative models for PDE distributions. Proves Hölder continuity of optimal transport and derives excess-risk bounds for DeepParticle learning.
