# The information-theoretic complexity of differentiable functions

**Autori:** Matthijs Ruijgrok · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.17801) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.17801)

## Abstract

A complexity measure for differentiable functions based on piecewise constant approximations is introduced, called V-complexity. This measure considers approximation quality and interval count. V-complexity is compared to compression-based complexity measures and hypothesized equivalent under Run Length Encoding and Lempel Ziv 77 algorithms. The measure contributes to defining Effective Complexity of complex systems when regularities are described by differentiable functions. Application to diffusion modeling shows V-complexity increases then decreases as equilibrium is reached.
