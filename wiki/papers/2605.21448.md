# A Note on EFX Inapproximability for Chores

**Autori:** Vasilis Christoforidis · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21448) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21448)

## Abstract

Studies approximability of EFX allocations for indivisible chores under complement-free cost functions. Provides explicit constant-factor inapproximability results for submodular and subadditive functions, narrowing the gap between lower and upper bounds through refined counterexamples.
