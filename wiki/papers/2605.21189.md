# Martin's Axiom, Large Continuum and Global Sigma1n-Uniformization

**Autori:** Stefan Hoffelner · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21189) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21189)

## Abstract

A generic extension of L is constructed satisfying Martin's Axiom, 2^ℵ₀=ℵ₃, a lightface Δ¹₃ wellorder of the reals, and Σ¹ₙ-uniformization for every n≥2 simultaneously.
