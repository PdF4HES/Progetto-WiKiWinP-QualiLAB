# Contradiction Graphs Determine VC Dimension

**Autori:** Jesse Campbell, Daniel Ibaibarriaga, Lev Reyzin · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20434) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20434)

## Abstract

The authors demonstrate that a single contradiction graph determines the threshold predicate VCdim(H)≥m and that the full sequence determines exact VC dimension, answering a question about detecting finite versus infinite VC dimension.
