# Higher-Rank Connections and Deformed Schrödinger Operators

**Autori:** Jonah Baerman, Alba Grassi, Giovanni Ravazzini · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20338) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20338)

## Abstract

Connection problems for order N linear differential equations related to the Baxter equation of quantum Toda chain are studied. The N-dimensional solution space yields multiple linearly independent decaying solutions at singularities. Weakest quantization conditions compatible with decaying behavior are derived and formulated via monodromy data. Results confirm topological string/spectral theory duality predictions for deformed Schrödinger equations.
