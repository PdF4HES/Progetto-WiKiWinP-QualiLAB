# A Serre type vanishing property of the twisted primitive cohomology

**Autori:** Hao Zhuang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21410) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21410)

## Abstract

A Serre type vanishing property is proven for the twisted primitive cohomology of a symplectic manifold, based on Tseng and Zhou's vanishing property under symplectic flatness. These properties emphasize the necessity of symplectic flatness when generalizing results from sheaf cohomology in complex geometry to primitive cohomology in symplectic geometry.
