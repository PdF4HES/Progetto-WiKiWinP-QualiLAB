# Modified Painlevé systems with meromorphic solutions for polynomial Hamiltonians of all degrees

**Autori:** Marta Dell'Atti, Thomas Kecker · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21166) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21166)

## Abstract

Non-autonomous Hamiltonian systems polynomial in two dependent variables with meromorphic solutions in the complex plane are reviewed. Using geometric approaches and Okamoto's spaces of initial conditions, differential constraints on coefficient functions are obtained. Newton polygon analysis yields all systems with polynomial Hamiltonians of degrees three through seven, up to affine equivalence. A list of 12 standard polynomial Hamiltonians serves as reference for the Painlevé equivalence problem, including previously unknown quartic and quintic variants.
