# Prototype-Guided Latent Alignment for Data-Efficient Fine-Tuning of Molecular Foundation Models

**Autori:** Rushikesh Pawar, Harshit Rawat, Ayush Kumar et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29969) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29969)

## Abstract

Proposes prototype-based alignment approach for data-efficient fine-tuning of machine learning interatomic potentials. Method identifies structural similarities between source and target domains, improving generalization in low-data regimes by up to 18% energy MAE reduction.
