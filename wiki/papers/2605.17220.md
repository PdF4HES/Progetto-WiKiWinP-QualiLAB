# Skewed weak and Pareto-tailed strong interactions accompany community diversity and complexity

**Autori:** Takuya Hojo, Taiko Arakaki, Koichi Fujimoto · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.17220) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.17220)

## Abstract

Analysis of ecological networks revealing interaction strength distributions with skewed weak and Pareto-strong tails (SWAPS). Using plant-animal datasets and Lotka-Volterra simulations, demonstrates that taxonomic conservatism and multiple interaction types generate SWAPS distributions accompanying community diversity and complexity increases.
