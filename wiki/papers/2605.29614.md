# Characterization of Chaotic and Homogeneous coexisting dynamics of a Memristive Thermo-Controlled MEMS

**Autori:** N. G. Koudafokê, Thierry Njougouo, Hilda A. Cerdeira et al. · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29614) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29614)

## Abstract

This work presents mathematical modeling and numerical investigation of a thermo-controlled Micro-Electro-Mechanical System obtained by coupling an HP memristor with mechanical and electrical resonators. Nonlinear electromechanical dynamics are analyzed through Lyapunov exponents, bifurcation diagrams, phase portraits, recurrence plots, Poincaré sections, and Fourier spectra. Results reveal parameter-dependent transitions between quasi-periodic and chaotic oscillations and signatures of coexisting dynamical regimes.
