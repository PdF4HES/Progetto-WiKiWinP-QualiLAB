# Information-Theoretic Appraisal of Electron Densities

**Autori:** Abdulrahman Y. Zamani, Kevin Carter-Fenk · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21105) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21105)

## Abstract

An information-theoretic assessment of atomic and molecular densities in ground state and under excitation, confinement, and ensemblization scenarios. J-divergence serves as a metric for benchmarking electron densities against coupled cluster and configuration interaction references. The work examines mean-field orbital information, Brueckner and Dyson orbitals, and entropic measures of electron delocalization.
