# A Regularized Shallow Water System

**Autori:** Evgueni Dinvay, Henrik Kalisch · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30044) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30044)

## Abstract

Introduces a regularized shallow-water system in which the nonlinear terms are modified by a bounded operator, preventing shock formation while maintaining consistency with long-wave assumptions.
