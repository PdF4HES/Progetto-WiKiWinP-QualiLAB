# The complex property of the boundary operator on simplicial complexes

**Autori:** Philipp Bartmann, Matthias Keller · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21069) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21069)

## Abstract

The complex property ∂∂ = 0 of the boundary operator ∂ on a weighted, infinite, and possibly non-locally finite simplicial complex is studied. Characterization in ℓ² in terms of the recurrence of the links of simplices is given. The property ensures that Hodge Laplacians Δ^H indeed act as δ∂ + ∂δ and allows definition of relative cohomology classes and harmonic Dirichlet eigenforms.
