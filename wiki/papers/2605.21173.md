# Multiple mixing and multiple fractional cohomological equation: semisimple setting

**Autori:** Zhenqi Jenny Wang · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21173) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21173)

## Abstract

An effective approach to higher-order mixing in the semisimple setting is developed. Effective exponential mixing of all orders is proven for partially hyperbolic algebraic actions, introducing a new fractional-cohomological method addressing solvability theory for multiple fractional cohomological equations.
