# The Hitchin and Knizhnik-Zamolodchikov connections are projectively equivalent in the genus zero case

**Autori:** Jørgen Ellegaard Andersen, Tim Henke · **2026** · [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20097) · [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20097)

## Abstract

Establishes projective equivalence between Knizhnik-Zamolodchikov and Hitchin connections in genus 0 with marked points, demonstrating that Pauly's isomorphism intertwines these connections up to scalar-valued one-forms.
